# Dung Gate

## Overview
- **Type:** City gate
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
The Dung Gate was one of the gates on [[Nehemiah]]'s nighttime inspection route and part of the wall repair circuit. It likely served the southern area associated with refuse removal from the city.
