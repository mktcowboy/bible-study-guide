# Araunah's Threshing Floor

## Overview
- **Type:** Threshing floor / altar site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Traditionally associated with the future temple mount area

## Biblical Significance
This is the site where the plague triggered by [[David's Census]] is stopped. [[David]] insists on buying the place rather than offering to God what costs him nothing. In [[1 Chronicles]], the owner is called [[Ornan the Jebusite]], and the site becomes explicitly tied to [[Temple Preparations by David]].
