# Horse Gate

## Overview
- **Type:** City gate
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem, likely near the eastern or southeastern wall

## Biblical Significance
The Horse Gate appears in Nehemiah's wall-rebuilding list near the priestly repair sections. The priests repaired above the Horse Gate, each working opposite his own house, making the gate part of the detailed communal restoration of Jerusalem.

The gate is also mentioned in connection with royal and temple geography elsewhere in the Old Testament, but Nehemiah emphasizes the shared labor of rebuilding.
