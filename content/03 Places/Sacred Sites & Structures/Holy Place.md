# Holy Place

## Overview
- **Type:** Tabernacle chamber
- **Testament:** Old
- **Primary Books:** [[Exodus]], [[Leviticus]]

## Biblical Significance
The Holy Place was the outer chamber inside the [[Tabernacle]], entered regularly by the priests. It contained the lampstand, the table of showbread, and the altar of incense.

## Key Events
- [[Priesthood Ordination]] — Priestly service was established
- [[Day of Atonement Established]] — The high priest moved from the Holy Place into the inner sanctuary once each year

## Key People Associated
- [[Aaron]] — First high priest
- [[Levites]] — Priestly tribe serving around the sanctuary
