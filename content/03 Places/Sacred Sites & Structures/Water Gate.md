# Water Gate

## Overview
- **Type:** City gate / public square
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem, exact ancient location debated

## Biblical Significance
The Water Gate is the public setting where [[Ezra]] read the Law to the returned community in Nehemiah 8. The open square before the gate became a place of hearing, explanation, repentance, and joy.
