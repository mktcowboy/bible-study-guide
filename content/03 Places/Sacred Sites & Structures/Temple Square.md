# Temple Square

## Overview
- **Type:** Public assembly space
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
The open space before the temple became the gathering place for Ezra's covenant assembly during the intermarriage crisis. The people stood there trembling because of the seriousness of the matter and the heavy rain.
