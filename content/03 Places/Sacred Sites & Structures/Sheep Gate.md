# Sheep Gate

## Overview
- **Type:** City gate
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
The Sheep Gate was rebuilt by Eliashib the high priest and the priests at the beginning of Nehemiah's wall roster. Its priestly association likely reflects its location near the temple and the movement of sacrificial animals.
