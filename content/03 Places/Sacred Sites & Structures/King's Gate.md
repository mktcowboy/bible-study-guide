# King's Gate

## Overview
- **Type:** Palace gate / administrative location
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Shushan / Susa, southwestern Iran

## Biblical Significance
The king's gate in [[Shushan]] was the palace threshold where officials gathered and where [[Mordecai]] served. It becomes a recurring stage in Esther: Mordecai sits there, refuses to bow to [[Haman]], mourns there after the decree, and later returns there in honor.

## Key Events
- [[Mordecai Discovers Plot]] - Mordecai hears of the conspiracy
- [[Genocide Decree]] - Haman's conflict with Mordecai begins at the gate
- [[Mordecai's Plea]] - Mordecai mourns near the gate
- [[Mordecai Honored]] - Mordecai's status is reversed
