# Ebenezer

## Overview
- **Type:** Memorial site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Mizpah and Shen in the hill-country battle zone

## Biblical Significance
Ebenezer first appears near Israel's defeat and the capture of the ark, but in 1 Samuel 7 it becomes the name of Samuel's memorial stone after the LORD defeats the Philistines.

## Key Events
- [[Ark Captured]] - Battlefield region of Israel's earlier humiliation
- [[Samuel's Judgeship]] - Samuel sets up the Ebenezer stone

## Key People Associated
- [[Samuel]] - Names the memorial
- [[Saul]] - Later reign shaped by the spiritual legacy Samuel leaves

## Important Verses
> "Then Samuel took a stone... and called the name of it Ebenezer, saying, Hitherto hath the LORD helped us." - 1 Samuel 7:12

## Notes
Ebenezer captures the reversal at the heart of early 1 Samuel: the place of defeat becomes the place of remembered help.
