# Temple Mount

## Overview
- **Type:** Sacred site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
The Temple Mount is the site associated with the temple in Jerusalem. In Ezra, the returned exiles lay the foundation of the second temple there, marking the restoration of central worship after exile.
