# Ahasuerus's Court

## Overview
- **Type:** Royal court / palace complex
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Shushan / Susa, southwestern Iran

## Biblical Significance
Ahasuerus's court is the royal environment where the Esther narrative unfolds: feasts, decrees, access protocols, banquets, and sudden reversals. It is a place of immense danger because royal words become empire-wide law, but also the place where Esther's courage is used for deliverance.
