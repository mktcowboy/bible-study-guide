# Samaria

## Overview
- **Type:** Capital city of the northern kingdom
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Founded by Omri in [[1 Kings]], Samaria becomes the political center of Israel's northern monarchy and the setting for much of the [[Ahab]] and [[Elijah]] conflict. In [[2 Kings]] it remains central through siege, prophecy, dynastic upheaval, and eventually the northern kingdom's collapse.
