# Judah (territory)

## Overview
- **Type:** Tribal Territory
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Southern hill country and surrounding regions of ancient Israel

## Biblical Significance
Judah is the first tribe named in Judges' post-Joshua campaigns and later becomes the royal tribe of David.
