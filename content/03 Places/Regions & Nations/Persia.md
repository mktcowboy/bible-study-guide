# Persia

## Overview
- **Type:** Empire
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Centered in modern Iran, extending across the ancient Near East

## Biblical Significance
Persia was the imperial power that conquered Babylon and allowed the Jewish exiles to return. Under [[Cyrus]], the first return began; under Darius I, the temple rebuilding was confirmed; under Artaxerxes, Ezra and Nehemiah received royal authorization for reform and rebuilding.

The book of [[Esther]] shows Jewish life in the Persian diaspora, especially at [[Shushan]], where providence preserved the people even far from Jerusalem.
