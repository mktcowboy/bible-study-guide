# Asia Minor

## Overview
- **Type:** Region
- **Testament:** New
- **Modern Location:** Turkey

## Biblical Significance
Asia Minor was a major arena of early Christian mission and pastoral correspondence. 1 Peter addresses believers scattered through Pontus, Galatia, Cappadocia, Asia, and Bithynia, presenting them as elect exiles living faithfully among the nations.

- [[Living Hope]] - Hope addressed to scattered believers
- [[Fiery Trial]] - Suffering faced by Christians in the region

## Key People Associated
- [[Peter]] - Wrote to believers in the region
