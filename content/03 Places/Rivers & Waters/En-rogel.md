# En-rogel

## Overview
- **Type:** Spring / gathering place
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
En-rogel is the site near [[Jerusalem]] where [[Adonijah]] gathers supporters for his attempted self-coronation. In [[1 Kings]] it becomes the failed rival stage to [[Gihon]].
