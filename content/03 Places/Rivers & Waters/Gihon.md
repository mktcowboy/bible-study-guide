# Gihon

## Overview
- **Type:** Spring / royal anointing site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** On the eastern slope of ancient Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
Gihon is the spring where [[Zadok]] and [[Nathan]] publicly anoint [[Solomon]] king in [[Solomon's Succession]], turning David's private promise into a public coronation.
