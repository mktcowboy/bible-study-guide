# Ahava River

## Overview
- **Type:** River / gathering place
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Uncertain, likely in Babylonia

## Biblical Significance
The Ahava River was the assembly point where [[Ezra]] gathered the second return group, reviewed the company, sought Levites, and proclaimed a fast for protection before the journey to Jerusalem.
