# Bochim

## Overview
- **Type:** Covenant Site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Uncertain; associated with the early Judges setting

## Biblical Significance
Bochim means "weepers" and is named after Israel weeps under the angel of the LORD's rebuke.

## Key Events
- [[Angel of the LORD at Bochim]] — Israel is rebuked for covenant compromise

## Key People Associated
- [[God|The LORD]] — Speaks rebuke through His angel
