# Cave of Adullam

## Overview
- **Type:** Wilderness refuge
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** In the Judean foothills near Adullam

## Biblical Significance
The cave becomes David's early stronghold while fleeing Saul. It is where the distressed, indebted, and discontented gather around him.

## Key Events
- [[David's Flight]] - David hides and gathers followers

## Key People Associated
- [[David]] - Takes refuge there
- [[Jonathan]] - Supports David during the wilderness years

## Important Verses
> "David therefore departed thence, and escaped to the cave Adullam..." - 1 Samuel 22:1

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]

## Notes
Adullam is the seedbed of David's kingdom: a cave full of outcasts becomes the beginning of a loyal company.
