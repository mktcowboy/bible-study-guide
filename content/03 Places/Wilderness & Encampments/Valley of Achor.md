# Valley of Achor

## Overview
- **Type:** Valley
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Jericho; exact location debated

## Biblical Significance
The Valley of Achor is where Achan is judged after taking devoted things from Jericho. Its name is associated with trouble.

## Key Events
- [[Sin of Achan]] — Achan and the stolen devoted things are judged

## Key People Associated
- [[Achan]] — His sin gives the valley its name
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leads the judgment

## Important Verses
> "Wherefore the name of that place was called, The valley of Achor, unto this day." — Joshua 7:26

## Cross-References
- [[Joshua]]
- Hosea 2:15

## Notes
Hosea later turns Achor into a sign of hope, showing God can transform a place of trouble into a door of restoration.
