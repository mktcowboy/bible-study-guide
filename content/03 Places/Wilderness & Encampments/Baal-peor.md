# Baal-peor

## Overview
- **Type:** Moabite cult site / region
- **Testament:** Old
- **Primary Book:** [[Numbers]]

## Biblical Significance
Baal-peor was the place associated with Israel's idolatry and sexual immorality with Moabite women. The incident brought a deadly plague until Phinehas acted zealously for the Lord.

## Key Events
- [[Baal Peor]] — Israel's apostasy and Phinehas's intervention

## Key People Associated
- [[Balaam]] — Later associated with the counsel that led Israel into sin
- [[Phinehas]] — Turned away wrath through zeal for the covenant

## Notes
Baal-peor becomes a shorthand for covenant betrayal at the edge of the promised land.
