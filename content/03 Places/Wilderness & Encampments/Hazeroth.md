# Hazeroth

## Overview
- **Type:** Wilderness encampment
- **Testament:** Old
- **Primary Book:** [[Numbers]]

## Biblical Significance
Hazeroth was a wilderness station after Kibroth-hattaavah. Miriam and Aaron challenged Moses there, and Miriam was struck with leprosy before being restored.

## Key Events
- [[Miriam and Aaron Challenge Moses]] — Moses' prophetic authority was vindicated
