# En-gedi

## Overview
- **Type:** Wilderness stronghold / oasis
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Western shore of the Dead Sea

## Biblical Significance
En-gedi is the wilderness refuge where [[David]] spared [[Saul]] in the cave rather than seizing the kingdom by violence.

## Key Events
- [[David's Flight]] - David spares Saul there

## Key People Associated
- [[David]] - Refuses vengeance
- [[Saul]] - Learns David has spared him

## Important Verses
> "Then David arose, and cut off the skirt of Saul's robe privily." - 1 Samuel 24:4

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]

## Notes
En-gedi shows David's restraint under pressure. The future king proves his heart before he ever takes the throne.
