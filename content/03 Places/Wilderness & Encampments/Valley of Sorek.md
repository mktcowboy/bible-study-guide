# Valley of Sorek

## Overview
- **Type:** Valley
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Between the hill country and Philistine plain

## Biblical Significance
The Valley of Sorek is the setting for Delilah's betrayal of Samson.

## Key Events
- [[Samson's Downfall]] — Delilah learns the secret of Samson's strength

## Key People Associated
- [[Samson]] — Betrayed here
- [[Delilah]] — Lives in the valley

## Important Verses
> "And it came to pass afterward, that he loved a woman in the valley of Sorek, whose name was Delilah." — Judges 16:4

## Notes
Sorek becomes the place where Samson's inner weakness is exposed.
