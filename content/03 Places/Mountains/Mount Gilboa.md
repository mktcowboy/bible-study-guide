# Mount Gilboa

## Overview
- **Type:** Mountain range / battlefield
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Southeast of the Jezreel Valley

## Biblical Significance
Mount Gilboa is the site of Saul's final defeat and death along with Jonathan and Saul's sons.

## Key Events
- [[Death of Saul & Jonathan]] - Israel's first king falls there

## Key People Associated
- [[Saul]] - Dies on Gilboa
- [[Jonathan]] - Falls in battle
- [[David]] - Later laments the tragedy

## Important Verses
> "And the battle went sore against Saul... and Saul was sore wounded..." - 1 Samuel 31:3

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]
- [[2 Samuel]]

## Notes
Gilboa closes Saul's reign in defeat and grief, setting up the transition to David in 2 Samuel.
