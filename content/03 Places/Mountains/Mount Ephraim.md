# Mount Ephraim

## Overview
- **Type:** Hill Country Region
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Central hill country of Israel

## Biblical Significance
Mount Ephraim appears repeatedly in Judges, especially in the appendix stories of Micah and the Danite migration.

## Key Events
- [[Micah's Idolatry]] — Micah's shrine is located in Mount Ephraim
- [[Danite Migration]] — Danite scouts pass through and steal the shrine

## Key People Associated
- [[Micah of Ephraim]] — Lives in the region
- [[Levites]] — A Levite serves at Micah's shrine

## Important Verses
> "And there was a man of mount Ephraim, whose name was Micah." — Judges 17:1

- [[Joshua]]

## Notes
The region becomes a stage for Israel's decentralized and confused worship.
