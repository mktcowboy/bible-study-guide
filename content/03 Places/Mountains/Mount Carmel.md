# Mount Carmel

## Overview
- **Type:** Mountain ridge / prophetic confrontation site
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Mount Carmel is where [[Elijah]] confronts the prophets of Baal before [[Ahab]] and all Israel. Fire from heaven there publicly answers the question of who is truly God.
