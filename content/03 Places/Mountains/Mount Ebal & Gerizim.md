# Mount Ebal & Gerizim

## Overview
- **Type:** Mountains / Covenant Site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Shechem in the central hill country

## Biblical Significance
Mount Ebal and Mount Gerizim are the mountains where Israel publicly rehearses covenant curses and blessings.

## Key Events
- [[Battle of Ai]] — Covenant reading follows the victory
- [[Blessings and Curses Proclaimed]] — Moses commands this ceremony before Israel enters the land

## Key People Associated
- [[Moses]] — Commands the ceremony in Deuteronomy
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Carries it out in Joshua

## Important Verses
> "Afterward he read all the words of the law, the blessings and cursings..." — Joshua 8:34

## Cross-References
- [[Deuteronomy]]
- [[Joshua]]

## Notes
The ceremony places the conquest under the authority of the covenant, not mere possession of land.
