# Mount Tabor

## Overview
- **Type:** Mountain
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Lower Galilee

## Biblical Significance
Mount Tabor is the gathering place for Barak's army before the battle against Sisera.

## Key Events
- [[Deborah and Barak]] — Barak gathers ten thousand men at Tabor

## Key People Associated
- [[Deborah]] — Commands Barak to go
- [[Barak]] — Gathers the army
- [[Sisera]] — Defeated after Barak descends

## Important Verses
> "Go and draw toward mount Tabor, and take with thee ten thousand men..." — Judges 4:6

## Cross-References
- [[Judges]]

## Notes
Tabor marks the place where prophetic command becomes public action.
