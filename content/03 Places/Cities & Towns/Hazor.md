# Hazor

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Tell el-Qedah / Tel Hazor in northern Israel

## Biblical Significance
Hazor is the leading northern city in Joshua's campaign and later reappears as an oppressor in Judges.

## Key Events
- [[Northern Campaign]] — Jabin gathers northern kings against Israel

## Key People Associated
- [[Jabin]] — King of Hazor
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Burns the city in the conquest

## Important Verses
> "And Joshua at that time turned back, and took Hazor, and smote the king thereof..." — Joshua 11:10

## Cross-References
- [[Joshua]]
- [[Judges]]

## Notes
Hazor's recurrence in Judges shows that conquest victories still required generational faithfulness.
