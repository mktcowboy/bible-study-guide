# Ophrah

## Overview
- **Type:** Town
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Often associated with the territory of Manasseh; exact location debated

## Biblical Significance
Ophrah is Gideon's hometown, the place of his calling, altar destruction, and later ephod.

## Key Events
- [[Gideon's Calling]] — Gideon is called while hiding from Midian
- [[Gideon's Ephod]] — Gideon's ephod becomes a snare

## Key People Associated
- [[Gideon]] — Judge from Ophrah
- [[Abimelech]] — Gideon's son

## Important Verses
> "Then Gideon built an altar there unto the LORD, and called it Jehovahshalom..." — Judges 6:24

## Cross-References
- [[Judges]]

## Notes
Ophrah holds both Gideon's faithful beginning and compromised ending.
