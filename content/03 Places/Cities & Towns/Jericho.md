# Jericho

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near modern Jericho in the Jordan Valley

## Biblical Significance
Jericho is the first major city Israel faces after crossing the Jordan. Its fall demonstrates that the conquest belongs to the LORD. In [[2 Kings]] it reappears at the opening of [[Elisha]]'s ministry, where bitter waters are healed and prophetic succession is publicly confirmed.

- [[Spies Sent to Jericho]] — Rahab hides the spies
