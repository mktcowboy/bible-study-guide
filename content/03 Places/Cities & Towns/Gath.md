# Gath

## Overview
- **Type:** Philistine city
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** One of the five chief Philistine cities

## Biblical Significance
Gath is home to [[Goliath]] and later becomes a place where [[David]] sought temporary asylum while fleeing Saul.

## Key Events
- [[David & Goliath]] - Goliath identified as the champion of Gath
- [[David's Flight]] - David later seeks refuge among the Philistines

## Key People Associated
- [[Goliath]] - Champion from Gath
- [[David]] - Flees there in danger

## Important Verses
> "And there went out a champion out of the camp of the Philistines, named Goliath, of Gath..." - 1 Samuel 17:4

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]

## Notes
Gath moves from the place of giant defiance to the uneasy refuge of God's anointed fugitive.
