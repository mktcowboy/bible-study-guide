# Sodom

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Dead Sea

## Biblical Significance
Sodom was the most wicked city of its generation. [[Lot]] dwelt there. God destroyed it with fire and brimstone after [[Abraham]] interceded. Lot and his two daughters were rescued. Lot's wife looked back and became a pillar of salt.

Sodom became the ultimate symbol of divine judgment on immorality.
