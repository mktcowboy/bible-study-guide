# Ur of the Chaldeans

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Tell al-Muqayyar, southern Iraq

## Biblical Significance
Ur was a major Sumerian city and the birthplace of [[Abraham]], [[Sarah]], and [[Lot]]. From this center of civilization and idolatry, God called Abraham to leave "thy country, and thy kindred, and thy father's house."

## Key Events
- [[Call of Abraham]] — God's call to leave Ur

## Key People Associated
- [[Abraham]] — Called from Ur
- [[Sarah]] — Born in Ur
- [[Lot]] — Born in Ur

## Important Verses
