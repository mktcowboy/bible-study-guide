# Ziklag

## Overview
- **Type:** Town / military base
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Southern Judah / Philistine borderland

## Biblical Significance
Ziklag became David's base while living among the Philistines. It was later raided by Amalekites, prompting David's recovery campaign.

## Key Events
- [[David's Flight]] - David lives there under Philistine shelter

## Key People Associated
- [[David]] - Uses Ziklag as a base
- [[Abigail]] - Part of David's household during this period

## Important Verses

## Notes
Ziklag is a place of tension and testing, where David learns to lead while still outside the throne.
