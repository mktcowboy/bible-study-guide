# Jerusalem (Zion)

## Overview
- **Type:** Stronghold / poetic name for Jerusalem
- **Testament:** Both
- **Modern Location:** The fortified ridge identified with the city of David

## Biblical Significance
Zion is the stronghold David captured from the Jebusites and made central to his kingdom. In later Scripture it becomes a rich theological name for God's chosen royal city.
