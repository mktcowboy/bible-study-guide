# Hebron

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Hebron, West Bank

## Biblical Significance
Hebron is one of the oldest cities in Canaan and the burial place of the patriarchs. It also becomes an important royal setting, since [[David]] first reigns there and in [[1 Chronicles]] all Israel gathers there to recognize his kingship.
