# Babel (Babylon)

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near modern Baghdad, Iraq

## Biblical Significance
Babel (Babylon) was built by [[Nimrod]] after the [[Flood]]. The name means "confusion" in Hebrew, connecting it to the Tower of Babel story. At Babel, united humanity attempted to build a city and tower "whose top may reach unto heaven" to make a name for themselves and avoid being scattered.

God confused their language, scattering them across the earth — the origin of linguistic diversity. Babel became a symbol of human pride and rebellion against God.

## Key Events
- [[Tower of Babel]] — Language confused, people scattered

## Key People Associated
- [[Noah]]'s descendants — scattered from Babel
