# Zarephath

## Overview
- **Type:** Phoenician town
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Zarephath is where God sustains [[Elijah]] through a widow during the drought. The place stands as a rebuke to unbelieving Israel and a sign that God's mercy reaches beyond covenant borders.
