# Ecbatana

## Overview
- **Type:** Persian royal archive city
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Hamadan, Iran

## Biblical Significance
Ecbatana is where Darius's officials found the memorandum of [[Cyrus]]'s original decree authorizing the rebuilding of the Jerusalem temple. That discovery led to renewed royal support for the project.
