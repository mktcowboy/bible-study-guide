# Tyre

## Overview
- **Type:** Phoenician coastal city
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** In present-day Lebanon

## Biblical Significance
Tyre is the home of [[Hiram of Tyre]] and a major trade partner in [[1 Kings]]. Through Tyre, cedar, craftsmen, and maritime skill flow into [[Solomon]]'s reign, while later Phoenician influence also reaches Israel through [[Jezebel]].
