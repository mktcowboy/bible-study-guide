# Dothan

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Dothan is the setting in [[2 Kings]] where [[Elisha]]'s servant sees the mountain full of heavenly horses and chariots of fire. The city becomes a vivid place of unseen protection.
