# Ramah

## Overview
- **Type:** Town
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Hill country region north of Jerusalem; exact references vary

## Biblical Significance
Ramah appears in the Benjamin region and becomes the home base of [[Samuel]]'s ministry. It is also where Israel's elders come to demand a king.

## Key Events
- [[War Against Benjamin]] - Region associated with the Benjamin conflict
- [[Demand for a King]] - Israel's elders gather to ask Samuel for a king
- [[Samuel's Judgeship]] - Samuel returns there as part of his circuit

## Key People Associated
- [[Samuel]] - Prophet and judge based there
- [[Saul]] - Encounters Samuel's leadership era through Ramah

## Important Verses
> "Then all the elders of Israel gathered themselves together, and came to Samuel unto Ramah." - 1 Samuel 8:4

## Cross-References
- [[Judges]]
- [[1 Samuel]]

## Notes
Ramah becomes one of the nerve centers of [[1 Samuel]]. The town is tied both to prophetic leadership and to Israel's pivotal turn toward monarchy.
