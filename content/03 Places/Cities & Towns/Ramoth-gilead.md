# Ramoth-gilead

## Overview
- **Type:** Fortified city / battle site
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Ramoth-gilead is the contested city that draws [[Ahab]] into the final battle foretold by [[Micaiah son of Imlah]]. It becomes the place where false confidence collapses under true prophecy.
