# Shechem

## Overview
- **Type:** City / covenant site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near modern Nablus

## Biblical Significance
Shechem is a major covenant location, associated with the patriarchs and [[Covenant Renewal at Shechem]]. In [[1 Kings]] it becomes the political gathering point where [[Rehoboam]]'s folly triggers [[Division of the Kingdom]].
