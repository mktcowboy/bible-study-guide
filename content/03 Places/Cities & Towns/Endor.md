# Endor

## Overview
- **Type:** Town
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near the Jezreel Valley

## Biblical Significance
Endor is remembered for Saul's desperate nighttime visit to a medium after the LORD no longer answered him.

## Key Events
- [[Saul & the Witch of Endor]] - Saul seeks forbidden counsel there

## Key People Associated
- [[Saul]] - Goes in disguise
- [[Samuel]] - Appears in the prophetic message of judgment

## Important Verses
> "Then said Saul unto his servants, Seek me a woman that hath a familiar spirit..." - 1 Samuel 28:7

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]
- Deuteronomy 18:10-12

## Notes
Endor marks the spiritual bottom of Saul's story: the king who once was chosen by God now seeks a word among the dead.
