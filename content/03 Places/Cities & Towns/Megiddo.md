# Megiddo

## Overview
- **Type:** City / battlefield
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Tel Megiddo, northern Israel

## Biblical Significance
Megiddo was a strategic city in the Jezreel Valley and the place where King [[Josiah]] was fatally wounded by Pharaoh Necho. His death marked a turning point after which Judah's final decline accelerated toward exile.
