# Nob

## Overview
- **Type:** Priestly city
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Jerusalem in Benjamin's territory

## Biblical Significance
Nob is where [[Ahimelech]] aided [[David]] with holy bread and Goliath's sword. It later became the site of one of Saul's darkest crimes when the priests were slaughtered.

## Key Events
- [[David's Flight]] - David receives bread and a sword there

## Key People Associated
- [[Ahimelech]] - Priest of Nob
- [[David]] - Receives help while fleeing
- [[Doeg the Edomite]] - Carries out the massacre

## Important Verses
> "Then came David to Nob to Ahimelech the priest..." - 1 Samuel 21:1

## Cross-References
- [[1 Samuel]]

## Notes
Nob turns a moment of priestly mercy into a witness against Saul's increasingly violent rule.
