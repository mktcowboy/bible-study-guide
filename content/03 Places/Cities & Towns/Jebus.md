# Jebus

## Overview
- **Type:** Pre-Israelite city stronghold
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Jebus is the earlier Jebusite stronghold that [[David]] captures and transforms into his royal city. In [[1 Chronicles]], the city's capture helps frame the David-centered retelling of kingship and worship.
