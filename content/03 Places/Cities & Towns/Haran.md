# Haran

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Edessa, southeastern Turkey

## Biblical Significance
Haran was where Abraham's father Terah stopped during the journey from [[Ur]] to [[Canaan]]. Abraham continued after Terah's death. Later, [[Isaac]] found [[Rebekah]] here, and [[Jacob]] fled here for 20 years.

## Key Events
- Terah's death
- Rebekah found at well
- Jacob's flight and service to Laban

## Key People Associated
- [[Abraham]] — Passed through
- [[Isaac]] — Found Rebekah here
- [[Rebekah]] — Lived here
- [[Jacob]] — Lived here 20 years
