# Bethel

## Overview
- **Type:** City / sacred site
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near modern Beitin

## Biblical Significance
Bethel was where [[Jacob]] saw the stairway to heaven and named the place "house of God." In [[1 Kings]] the same site becomes one of [[Jeroboam]]'s rival worship centers and a symbol of corrupted devotion.
