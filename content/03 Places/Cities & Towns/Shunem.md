# Shunem

## Overview
- **Type:** Town
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Shunem is the home of the woman who extends hospitality to [[Elisha]] and later receives her son back from death. The town becomes one of the clearest settings of prophetic compassion in [[2 Kings]].
