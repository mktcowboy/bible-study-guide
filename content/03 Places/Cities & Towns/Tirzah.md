# Tirzah

## Overview
- **Type:** Royal city
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Tirzah serves as an early royal center in the northern kingdom before Samaria rises to prominence. It appears repeatedly in the unstable succession cycles following [[Jeroboam]].
