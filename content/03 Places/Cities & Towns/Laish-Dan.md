# Laish-Dan

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Tel Dan in northern Israel

## Biblical Significance
Laish is conquered by the tribe of Dan and renamed Dan. It becomes a northern location associated with idolatrous worship.

## Key Events
- [[Danite Migration]] — Danites capture Laish and set up Micah's image

## Key People Associated
- [[Micah of Ephraim]] — His idol is taken there
- [[Levites]] — A Levite serves the Danite shrine

## Important Verses
> "And they called the name of the city Dan..." — Judges 18:29

## Cross-References
- [[Judges]]
- [[1 Kings]]

## Notes
Dan's later association with idolatry in 1 Kings has roots in this Judges episode.
