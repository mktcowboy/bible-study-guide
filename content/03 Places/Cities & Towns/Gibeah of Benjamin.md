# Gibeah of Benjamin

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Benjaminite territory north of Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
Gibeah is the site of the atrocity that leads to civil war against Benjamin.

## Key Events
- [[The Levite and His Concubine]] — Atrocity occurs in Gibeah
- [[War Against Benjamin]] — City becomes the focus of judgment

## Key People Associated
- [[Benjamin]] — Tribe responsible for defending the city

## Important Verses
