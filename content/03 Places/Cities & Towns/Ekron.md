# Ekron

## Overview
- **Type:** Philistine city
- **Testament:** Old

## Biblical Significance
Ekron is the city associated with Baal-zebub in the opening chapter of [[2 Kings]]. It marks the spiritual absurdity of an Israelite king seeking a Philistine god.
