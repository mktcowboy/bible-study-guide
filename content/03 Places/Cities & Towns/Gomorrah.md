# Gomorrah

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Near Dead Sea (near Sodom)

## Biblical Significance
Destroyed alongside [[Sodom]] by fire and brimstone. Always mentioned together, "Sodom and Gomorrah" became shorthand for divine judgment.

## Key Events
- [[Destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah]]

## Key People Associated
- [[Lot]] — Rescued
- [[Abraham]] — Interceded

## Important Verses
- "Then the LORD rained upon Sodom and upon Gomorrah brimstone and fire from the LORD out of heaven." — Genesis 19:24
