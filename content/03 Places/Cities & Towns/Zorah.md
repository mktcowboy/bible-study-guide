# Zorah

## Overview
- **Type:** Town
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Shephelah region, associated with the tribe of Dan

## Biblical Significance
Zorah is Samson's hometown region and the place where the Spirit of the LORD begins to move him.

## Key Events
- [[Samson's Birth]] — Samson's family is from Zorah
- [[Samson's Exploits]] — Samson's early movements begin near Zorah

## Key People Associated
- [[Manoah]] — Samson's father
- [[Samson]] — Danite judge

## Important Verses
> "And the Spirit of the LORD began to move him at times in the camp of Dan between Zorah and Eshtaol." — Judges 13:25

## Notes
Zorah anchors Samson's story in Danite territory near Philistine pressure.
