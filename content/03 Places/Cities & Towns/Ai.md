# Ai

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Traditionally located near Bethel in the central hill country

## Biblical Significance
Ai is the place where Israel first suffers defeat in Canaan because of [[Achan]]'s hidden sin.

## Key Events
- [[Sin of Achan]] — Israel is defeated because devoted things are in the camp
- [[Battle of Ai]] — Ai is defeated after judgment and renewed obedience

## Key People Associated
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leads the battle
- [[Achan]] — His sin causes the first defeat

## Important Verses
> "And the men of Ai smote of them about thirty and six men..." — Joshua 7:5

## Cross-References
- [[Joshua]]
- [[Bethel]]

## Notes
Ai is a theological checkpoint: victory in the land cannot be separated from holiness among the people.
