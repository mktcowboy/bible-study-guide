# Gibeon

## Overview
- **Type:** City
- **Testament:** Old
- **Modern Location:** Often identified with el-Jib, northwest of Jerusalem

## Biblical Significance
Gibeon is a Hivite city whose inhabitants deceive Israel into a treaty, then become the reason for Israel's battle against a southern coalition. In the monarchy it also becomes a significant worship site, connected with the tabernacle in [[1 Chronicles]] and with [[Solomon's Wisdom]] in [[1 Kings]].
