# Apostles

## Overview
This hub focuses on the Twelve apostles appointed by [[Jesus Christ]] during his earthly ministry.

- [[Andrew]]
- [[James (son of Zebedee)]]
- [[John (Apostle)]]
- [[Philip (Apostle)]]
- [[Nathanael]]
- [[Matthew (Apostle)]]
- [[Thomas]]
- [[James (son of Alphaeus)]]
- [[Thaddaeus]]
- [[Simon the Zealot]]
- [[Judas Iscariot]]

## Key Books
- [[Matthew]]
- [[Mark]]

- [[John]]

## Notes
- This category is intentionally limited to the Twelve.
- Broader New Testament leaders and co-laborers belong in [[Early Church]].
- [[Paul (Apostle)]] is an apostle in the wider New Testament sense, but he is not one of the Twelve and therefore does not live in the Apostles folder.
