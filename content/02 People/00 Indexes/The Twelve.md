# The Twelve

## Overview
The Twelve were the core apostolic circle appointed by [[Jesus Christ]] during his earthly ministry.

## Biblical Significance
The Twelve represent the renewed people of God around Jesus, corresponding symbolically to Israel's twelve tribes and later serving as witnesses to his resurrection.

## Key Events
- [[Sending the Twelve]] — Jesus commissioned them for kingdom mission
- [[Last Supper]] — They shared the Passover meal before the crucifixion
