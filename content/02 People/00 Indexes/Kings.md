# Kings

## Overview
This hub gathers key royal figures in Israel, Judah, and surrounding nations. Kingship is one of the Bible's central themes because it raises questions of covenant faithfulness, justice, worship, and the hope of a greater coming king.

## Foundational Kings of Israel
- [[Saul]] - Israel's first king
- [[David]] - Covenant king and model royal figure
- [[Solomon]] - Builder of the temple and heir of David
