# Prophets

## Overview
Prophets are covenant messengers who speak God's word into moments of rebellion, crisis, reform, judgment, and hope. Some are attached to prophetic books, while others operate within historical narratives.

## Major Prophetic Figures
- [[Moses]] - Foundational prophet and covenant mediator
- [[Samuel]] - Prophet at the transition to monarchy
- [[Nathan]] - Court prophet who confronted David
- [[Elijah]] - Prophetic challenger to idolatry
- [[Elisha]] - Successor to Elijah with an expansive miracle ministry
- [[Isaiah]]
- [[Jeremiah]]
- [[Ezekiel]]
- [[Daniel]]
