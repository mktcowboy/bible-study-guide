# Ehud

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Left-handed judge from Benjamin
- **Family:** Son of Gera
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Ehud is raised up by the LORD when Israel cries out under Moabite oppression. He brings tribute to Eglon, king of Moab, then kills him with a concealed dagger and leads Israel to victory.

## Key Events
- [[Ehud's Assassination]] — Kills Eglon and breaks Moab's power

## Relationships
- [[Eglon]] — Moabite oppressor defeated by Ehud

## Important Verses
