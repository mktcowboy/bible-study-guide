# Jephthah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Gileadite judge and warrior
- **Family:** Son of Gilead
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Jephthah is rejected by his brothers but later recalled to lead Gilead against Ammon. He argues from Israel's history, defeats Ammon, and then suffers tragedy because of a rash vow.

## Key Events
- [[Jephthah's Vow]] — Vows rashly before battle and faces tragic consequences

## Relationships
- [[Minor Judges]] — Belongs to the later judge period
