# Minor Judges

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Briefly recorded judges of Israel
- **Family:** Tola, Jair, Ibzan, Elon, and Abdon
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
The minor judges receive shorter notices than Deborah, Gideon, Jephthah, or Samson, but they mark periods of leadership and relative stability in Israel's troubled history.

- [[Minor Judgeship Periods]] — Tola, Jair, Ibzan, Elon, and Abdon judge Israel
