# Shamgar

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Judge / deliverer
- **Family:** Son of Anath
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Shamgar receives only a brief notice, but his act is memorable: he kills six hundred Philistines with an ox goad and delivers Israel.

## Key Events
- [[Shamgar's Deliverance]] — Delivers Israel from Philistine pressure
