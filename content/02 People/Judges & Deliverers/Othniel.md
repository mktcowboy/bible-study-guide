# Othniel

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** First judge of Israel
- **Family:** Related to [[Caleb]]
- **Key Books:** [[Joshua]], [[Judges]]

## Biography
Othniel first appears as the warrior who captures Kiriath-sepher and receives Achsah, Caleb's daughter, as wife. In Judges, the Spirit of the LORD comes upon him, and he delivers Israel from Cushan-rishathaim.

## Key Events
- [[Othniel's Deliverance]] — Delivers Israel and brings forty years of rest

## Relationships
- [[Caleb]] — Older family leader connected to Othniel's story

## Important Verses
> "And the Spirit of the LORD came upon him, and he judged Israel..." — Judges 3:10

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Joshua]], [[Judges]]

## Notes
Othniel begins the judge cycle with the clearest pattern: oppression, Spirit-empowered deliverance, and rest.
