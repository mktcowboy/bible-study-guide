# Barak

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Military leader under Deborah's prophetic direction
- **Family:** Son of Abinoam
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Barak is summoned by [[Deborah]] to gather men at Mount Tabor and fight Sisera. He hesitates to go unless Deborah goes with him, and the honor of killing Sisera goes to [[Jael]].

## Key Events
- [[Deborah and Barak]] — Leads Israel's army against Sisera

## Relationships
- [[Deborah]] — Prophetess who summons him
- [[Sisera]] — Enemy commander defeated in the campaign
- [[Jael]] — Kills Sisera

## Important Verses
> "If thou wilt go with me, then I will go: but if thou wilt not go with me, then I will not go." — Judges 4:8

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Judges]], Hebrews 11:32

## Notes
Barak is flawed but faithful enough to appear in Hebrews 11.
