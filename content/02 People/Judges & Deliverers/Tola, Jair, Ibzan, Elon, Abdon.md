# Tola, Jair, Ibzan, Elon, Abdon

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Minor judges of Israel
- **Family:** Various tribes and regions
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
These judges receive shorter notices than Deborah, Gideon, Jephthah, or Samson, but they show that God preserved Israel through many leaders across many regions.

## Key Events
- Minor judges - Brief records of leadership and rest

## Relationships
- Israel - Judged and led by these figures between major deliverance narratives

## Important Verses
- Judges 10:1-5 - Tola and Jair judge Israel.
- Judges 12:8-15 - Ibzan, Elon, and Abdon judge Israel.

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Judges]]

## Notes
The minor judges remind the reader that God's care is not limited to the most dramatic stories.
