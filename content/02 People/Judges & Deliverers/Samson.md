# Samson

## Summary
Nazirite judge. Extraordinary strength. Killed lion. Defeated Philistines. Delivered to Delilah. Lost strength. Pulled down temple. Killed more at death than life.

## Key Passages
Judges 13-16, Hebrews 11:32, 1 Corinthians 9:10
