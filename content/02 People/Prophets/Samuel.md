# Samuel

## Summary
Last judge of Israel, prophet, and kingmaker. Given in answer to [[Hannah]]'s prayer, called by God as a child at [[Shiloh]], and used to anoint both [[Saul]] and [[David]]. Bridges the era of [[Judges]] and monarchy with unusual spiritual integrity.

## Key Passages
[[1 Samuel]] 1-12, Acts 3:24, Acts 13:20-22

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 3:10
- 1 Samuel 12:22

## Related People
[[Hannah]], [[Eli]], [[Saul]], [[David]]
