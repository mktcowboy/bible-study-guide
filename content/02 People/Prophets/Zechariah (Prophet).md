# Zechariah (Prophet)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Post-exilic prophet who encouraged temple rebuilding and renewed messianic hope
- **Family:** Son of Berechiah, son of Iddo
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]], [[Zechariah]]

## Biography
Zechariah prophesied alongside [[Haggai (Prophet)|Haggai]] during the renewed effort to rebuild the temple. His visions placed the restoration work in a larger theological frame of cleansing, priesthood, kingship, and the future coming of the LORD's king.
