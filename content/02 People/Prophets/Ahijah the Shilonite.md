# Ahijah the Shilonite

## Summary
Prophet from [[Shiloh]] who announces that God will tear most of the kingdom from [[Solomon]] and give it to [[Jeroboam]]. He later pronounces judgment on Jeroboam's house when the king turns his calling into idolatry.
