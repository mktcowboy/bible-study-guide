# Hosea (Prophet)

## Summary
Prophet. Married harlot Gomer. Picture of Gods love for unfaithful Israel. Children: Jezreel, Lo-ruhamah, Lo-ammi. Called Israel to return.

## Key Passages
Hosea, Matthew 1:21, 1 Corinthians 6:18-20, Ephesians 2:13, Romans 9:25-26

## Important Verses
- Hosea 1:2
- Hosea 14:1
