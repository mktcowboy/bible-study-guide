# Gad

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Prophet / seer associated with David
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[1 Samuel]], [[2 Samuel]], [[1 Chronicles]]

## Biography
Gad advised David during his fugitive years and later brought God's word after David's census. He is called David's seer.

## Key Events
- [[David's Flight]] - Gad tells David to leave the hold and go to Judah
- [[David's Census]] - Gad announces judgment and directs David to build an altar

## Relationships
- [[David]] - Counsels and confronts him

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 22:5 - Gad tells David to go into Judah.
- 2 Samuel 24:11-19 - Gad brings God's word after the census.

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[1 Samuel]], [[2 Samuel]], [[1 Chronicles]]

## Notes
Gad's ministry follows David from wilderness survival to royal accountability.
