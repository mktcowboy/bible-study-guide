# The Man with the Measuring Rod

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Angelic guide who measures Ezekiel's visionary temple
- **Family:** Heavenly/visionary figure
- **Key Books:** [[Ezekiel]]

## Biography
In Ezekiel's final temple vision, a man whose appearance was like bronze holds a linen cord and a measuring reed. He guides [[Ezekiel]] through the temple complex, measuring gates, courts, chambers, walls, and altar.

The measurements communicate order, holiness, and restored access to God's presence. The figure's work prepares for the return of the glory of the LORD to the temple.

- The river of healing
