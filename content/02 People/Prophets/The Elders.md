# The Elders

## Overview
The elders were recognized leaders and representatives among Israel's tribes, families, towns, and assemblies.

## Biblical Role
In the Pentateuch, elders appear as witnesses, judges, covenant representatives, and local leaders. They stand with Moses, help bear communal responsibility, and later participate in covenant ceremonies and legal proceedings.
