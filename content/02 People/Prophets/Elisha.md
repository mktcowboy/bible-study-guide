# Elisha

## Summary
Successor of [[Elijah]] who receives the requested double portion and becomes the central prophetic figure of [[2 Kings]]. [[Elisha]] heals waters at [[Jericho]], aids widows and kings, raises the son at [[Shunem]], heals [[Naaman]] in the [[Jordan River]], exposes armies at [[Dothan]], and continues speaking God's word deep into Israel's decline.
