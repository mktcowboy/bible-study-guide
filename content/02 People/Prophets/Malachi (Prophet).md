# Malachi (Prophet)

## Summary
Last OT prophet. Means My messenger. Priests corrupt. Stolen tithes. Divorce condemned. Elijah comes before day of Lord. Choice of blessing or curse.

## Key Passages
Malachi, Matthew 11:10, 17:12, Mark 1:2, Luke 7:27

## Important Verses
- Malachi 3:8
- Malachi 3:1
- Malachi 4:5
