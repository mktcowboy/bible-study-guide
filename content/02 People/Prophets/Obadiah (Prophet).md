# Obadiah (Prophet)

## Summary
Steward in [[Ahab]]'s house who feared the LORD even while serving inside a corrupt court. [[Obadiah (Prophet)|Obadiah]] protected a hundred prophets from [[Jezebel]] and helped arrange Elijah's return to Ahab during the drought.
