# Haggai (Prophet)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Post-exilic prophet who urged the returned community to resume temple rebuilding
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]], [[Haggai]]

## Biography
Haggai prophesied in the second year of Darius I, calling the returned exiles to stop neglecting the house of the LORD while building their own houses. His word stirred [[Zerubbabel]] and [[Jeshua]] to resume temple work.
