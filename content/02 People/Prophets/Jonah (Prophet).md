# Jonah (Prophet)

## Summary
Prophet who fled. Swallowed by fish. Preached to Nineveh. Angry at Gods mercy. Son of Amittai. From Gath-hepher. Sign for Christ.

## Key Passages
Jonah, 2 Kings 14:25, Matthew 12:39-41, Luke 11:29-32

## Important Verses
- Jonah 1:17
- Jonah 2:1
