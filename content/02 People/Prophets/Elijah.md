# Elijah

## Summary
Fire prophet from Tishbe who confronts [[Ahab]] and [[Jezebel]] during the darkest Baal crisis of the northern kingdom. [[Elijah]] announces the drought, is sustained in [[Zarephath]], defeats the prophets of Baal on [[Mount Carmel]], hears God at [[Mount Sinai|Horeb]], and becomes the defining prophetic voice of [[1 Kings]] before his later translation in [[2 Kings]].
