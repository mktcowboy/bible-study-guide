# Zedekiah son of Chenaanah

## Summary
Court prophet in [[1 Kings]] who theatrically promises victory to [[Ahab]] at [[Ramoth-gilead]] and strikes [[Micaiah son of Imlah]]. He represents the danger of prophetic performance without truth.
