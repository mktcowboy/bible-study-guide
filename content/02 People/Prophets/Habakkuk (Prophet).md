# Habakkuk (Prophet)

## Summary
Prophet who questioned God. Why evil? Why Babylon? Righteous shall live by faith. Trust despite circumstances. Prayer of praise.

## Key Passages
Habakkuk, Romans 1:17, Galatians 3:11, Hebrews 10:38
