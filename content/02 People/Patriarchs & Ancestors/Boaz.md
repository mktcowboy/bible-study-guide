# Boaz

## Summary
Wealthy Bethlehemite of the family of [[Elimelech]]. Showed covenant kindness to [[Ruth]], acted as kinsman-redeemer at the gate, married Ruth, and fathered [[Obed]]. Great-grandfather of [[David]] and a strong Old Testament picture of redemptive grace.

## Key Passages
Ruth, Matthew 1:5

## Important Verses
- Ruth 2:1
- Ruth 3:9
