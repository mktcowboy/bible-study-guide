# Sheshbazzar

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Prince of Judah entrusted with the returned temple vessels
- **Family:** Called a prince of Judah
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]]

## Biography
Sheshbazzar received the temple vessels from [[Cyrus]] after the decree authorizing return and rebuilding. He carried the vessels back toward [[Jerusalem]], symbolizing that the worship stolen by Babylon was being restored.

- [[First Return]]

## Relationships
- [[Cyrus]] - Entrusted him with the vessels
- [[Zerubbabel]] - Later central leader of the rebuilding community

## Important Verses

- Ezra 5:14-16

## Notes
The exact relationship between Sheshbazzar and Zerubbabel is debated, but the text presents Sheshbazzar as a key figure in the first movement of restoration.
