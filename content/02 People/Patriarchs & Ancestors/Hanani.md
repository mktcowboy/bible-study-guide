# Hanani

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Nehemiah's brother who brought news from Jerusalem
- **Family:** Brother of [[Nehemiah]]
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
Hanani came from Judah to [[Shushan]] and reported Jerusalem's broken walls and burned gates to [[Nehemiah]]. His report triggered Nehemiah's mourning, prayer, and eventual royal commission.
