# Mishael (Meshach)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Judean exile renamed Meshach in Babylon
- **Family:** One of Daniel's three companions
- **Key Books:** [[Daniel]]

## Biography
Mishael was renamed Meshach in Babylon. With [[Hananiah (Shadrach)|Hananiah]] and [[Azariah (Abednego)|Azariah]], he refused idolatrous worship and survived the fiery furnace by God's deliverance.
