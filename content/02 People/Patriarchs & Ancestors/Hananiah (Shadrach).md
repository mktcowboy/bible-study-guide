# Hananiah (Shadrach)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Judean exile renamed Shadrach in Babylon
- **Family:** One of Daniel's three companions
- **Key Books:** [[Daniel]]

## Biography
Hananiah was taken into Babylonian training and given the name Shadrach. Alongside [[Mishael (Meshach)|Mishael]] and [[Azariah (Abednego)|Azariah]], he refused to bow to Nebuchadnezzar's image and was delivered from the fiery furnace.
