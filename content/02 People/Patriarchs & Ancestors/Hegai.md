# Hegai

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Keeper of the women in Ahasuerus's court

## Biography
Hegai oversaw the women gathered in [[Ahasuerus's Court]] during the search for Vashti's replacement. [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]] found favor with him, and he provided her with special care before she was presented to [[Ahasuerus]].
