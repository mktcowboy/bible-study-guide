# Hatach

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Attendant who carried messages between Esther and Mordecai
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Hatach served [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]] in the palace and became the messenger between Esther and [[Mordecai]] when the genocide decree threw the Jews into mourning. Through him, Mordecai sent Esther the details of the decree and urged her to intercede.
