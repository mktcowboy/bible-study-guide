# Benaiah

## Summary
Son of Jehoiada, one of [[David]]'s mighty men and captain over the royal guard. He emerges as a decisive loyalist in the transition from David to [[Solomon]], standing with [[Zadok]] and [[Nathan]] and later executing judgment on [[Joab]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 8:18, 20:23, 23:20-23, [[1 Kings]] 1-2

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 23:20-23
- 1 Kings 1:36-37
- 1 Kings 2:34-35

## Related People
[[David]], [[Solomon]], [[Zadok]], [[Nathan]], [[Joab]], [[Bathsheba]]
