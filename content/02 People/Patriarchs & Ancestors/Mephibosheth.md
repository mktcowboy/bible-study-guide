# Mephibosheth

## Summary
Usually refers to the son of [[Jonathan]] and grandson of [[Saul]], whom [[David]] spares and honors for Jonathan's sake. His appearances in [[2 Samuel]] 9, 16, and 19 test covenant loyalty, while [[2 Samuel]] 21:8 also mentions another Mephibosheth, a son of [[Saul]] by [[Rizpah]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 4:4, 9, 16, 19, 21:7-8

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 9:1
- 2 Samuel 9:13
- 2 Samuel 19:24-30

## Related People
[[Jonathan]], [[Saul]], [[David]], [[Rizpah]], [[Absalom]]
