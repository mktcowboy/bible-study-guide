# Manoah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Father of Samson
- **Family:** Danite from Zorah
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Manoah and his wife receive the angelic announcement of Samson's birth. Manoah asks how the child should be raised, and the messenger repeats the Nazirite instructions.

## Key Events
- [[Samson's Birth]] — Receives the birth announcement with his wife
