# Obed

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Son of Ruth and Boaz; grandfather of David
- **Family:** Son of [[Ruth]] and [[Boaz]]
- **Key Books:** [[Ruth]], [[1 Chronicles]], [[Matthew]]

## Biography
Obed was born to Ruth and Boaz after Boaz redeemed Elimelech's line. The women of Bethlehem celebrated him as a restorer of Naomi's life. He became the father of Jesse and grandfather of David.

## Key Events
- [[Birth of Obed]] - Naomi's emptiness is turned to restoration

- [[David]] - Grandson

## Important Verses
- Ruth 4:17 - Obed is father of Jesse, father of David.
- Matthew 1:5 - Obed appears in the lineage of Christ.
