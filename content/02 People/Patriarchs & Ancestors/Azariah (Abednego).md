# Azariah (Abednego)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Judean exile renamed Abednego in Babylon
- **Family:** One of Daniel's three companions
- **Key Books:** [[Daniel]]

## Biography
Azariah was renamed Abednego as part of Babylon's attempt to reshape Judean identity. With [[Hananiah (Shadrach)|Hananiah]] and [[Mishael (Meshach)|Mishael]], he refused to worship Nebuchadnezzar's image and was preserved in the fiery furnace.
