# Jabez

## Summary
Briefly noted figure in the genealogies of [[1 Chronicles]] who becomes memorable because of his prayer for blessing, protection, and enlarged borders. [[Jabez]] stands out as a small but bright flash of dependence on God inside a long list of names.
