# Hushai

## Summary
Friend and loyal adviser of [[David]] who stays in Jerusalem during [[Absalom]]'s revolt to frustrate the counsel of [[Ahithophel]]. His successful counter-counsel in [[2 Samuel]] 17 is one of the key human means by which David is preserved.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 15-17

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 15:32-37
- 2 Samuel 17:5-14
- 2 Samuel 17:22
