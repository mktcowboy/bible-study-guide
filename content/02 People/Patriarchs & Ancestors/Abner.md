# Abner

## Summary
Commander of [[Saul]]'s army and son of Ner, Saul's uncle. After Saul's death he set up [[Ishbosheth]] over Israel and prolonged the struggle between Saul's house and [[David]]. His eventual move toward David might have unified the kingdom sooner, but he was murdered by [[Joab]] at [[Hebron]].

[[1 Samuel]] 14:50-51, 17:55-58, 26, [[2 Samuel]] 2-4
