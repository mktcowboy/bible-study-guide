# Hiram the Craftsman

## Summary
Skilled bronze worker from [[Tyre]] brought into [[Solomon]]'s service for the finishing work of the temple. [[Hiram the Craftsman|Hiram]] appears in [[1 Kings]] 7 as the artisan behind many of the temple's great vessels and pillars.
