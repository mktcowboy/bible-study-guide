# Job (Person)

## Summary
Righteous sufferer. Lost wealth, children, health. Friends accused him. Maintained innocence. God answered from whirlwind. Restored double. Patient endurer.

## Key Passages
Job, James 5:11, Zephaniah 2:9, Job 19:25
