# Caleb

## Summary
Son of Jephunneh. One of twelve spies. Faithful with Joshua. Inherited Hebron at 85. Ate from promise at 40 and 45. Valiant warrior. Tribe of Judah.

## Key Passages
Numbers 13-14, Deuteronomy 1, Joshua 14-15, Judges 1, 1 Chronicles 2, Hebrews 11:30
