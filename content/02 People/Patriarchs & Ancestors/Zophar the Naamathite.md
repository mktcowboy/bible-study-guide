# Zophar the Naamathite

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Third of Job's friends, blunt defender of retributive justice
- **Family:** Naamathite
- **Key Books:** [[Job]]

## Biography
Zophar is the harshest and least patient of the three friends. He argues that Job deserves even more punishment than he has received and calls him to repent so restoration can come.

Zophar speaks only in the first two cycles and falls silent in the third, a narrative sign that the friends' case has exhausted itself.
