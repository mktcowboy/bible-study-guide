# Amasa

## Summary
Cousin of [[Joab]] and [[Abishai]], first appointed by [[Absalom]] as commander of the rebel army and later chosen by [[David]] to replace Joab. His delayed response against [[Sheba]] and his murder at Joab's hand expose the fragile state of David's kingdom after Absalom's revolt.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 17:25, 19:13, 20:4-12, 1 Chronicles 2:16-17
