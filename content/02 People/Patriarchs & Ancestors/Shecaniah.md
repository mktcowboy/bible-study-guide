# Shecaniah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Israelite who proposed covenant action during Ezra's intermarriage crisis
- **Family:** Son of Jehiel
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]]

## Biography
Shecaniah responded to Ezra's public grief by acknowledging the community's sin and proposing a covenant to put away the unlawful foreign marriages. His words helped move the assembly from mourning to concrete reform.
