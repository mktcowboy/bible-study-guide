# Mahlon & Chilion

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Sons of Elimelech and Naomi
- **Family:** Sons of [[Elimelech]] and [[Naomi]]
- **Key Books:** [[Ruth]]

## Biography
Mahlon and Chilion moved from Bethlehem to Moab with their parents during famine. They married Moabite women, Ruth and Orpah, and both died in Moab, leaving Naomi without husband or sons.

## Key Events
- [[Deaths in Moab]] - Both sons die after marrying in Moab

## Relationships
- [[Elimelech]] - Father
- [[Naomi]] - Mother
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] - Wife of Mahlon
- Orpah - Wife of Chilion

## Important Verses
- Ruth 1:4-5 - Mahlon and Chilion marry and die in Moab.
- Ruth 4:10 - Boaz raises up the name of Mahlon through redemption.

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Ruth]]

## Notes
Their deaths create the emptiness that God will answer through Ruth's loyalty and Boaz's redemption.
