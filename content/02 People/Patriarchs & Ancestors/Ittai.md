# Ittai

## Summary
Gittite commander who shows remarkable loyalty to [[David]] during [[Absalom]]'s revolt. Though a foreigner, he models covenant faithfulness more clearly than many Israelites and is entrusted with part of David's army.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 15:19-22, 18:2, 5, 12

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 15:21
- 2 Samuel 18:2

## Related People
[[David]], [[Absalom]], [[Joab]]
