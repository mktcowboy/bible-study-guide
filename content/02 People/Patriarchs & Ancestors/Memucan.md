# Memucan

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Persian prince who advised Vashti's deposition
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Memucan was one of the seven Persian princes who saw the king's face. After [[Vashti]] refused [[Ahasuerus]]'s command, Memucan advised that her removal should be made an empire-wide decree.
