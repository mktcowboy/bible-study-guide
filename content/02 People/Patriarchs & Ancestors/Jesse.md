# Jesse

## Summary
Son of [[Obed]] and father of [[David]]. Though Ruth only names him in the closing genealogy, Jesse becomes the immediate link between the redemption story in Bethlehem and the rise of Israel's kingly line.

## Key Passages
Ruth 4:17-22, 1 Samuel 16, Isaiah 11:1

## Important Verses
- Ruth 4:17
- Isaiah 11:1

## Related People
[[Obed]], [[David]], [[Samuel]]
