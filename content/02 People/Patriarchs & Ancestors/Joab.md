# Joab

## Summary
Son of Zeruiah and commander of [[David]]'s army. He is one of the most effective and dangerous figures in [[2 Samuel]]: he kills [[Abner]], helps bring [[Absalom]] back to Jerusalem, slays Absalom against David's command, and later murders [[Amasa]] during the revolt of [[Sheba]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 2-3, 10-11, 14, 18-20, [[1 Kings]] 1-2

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 3:27
- 2 Samuel 18:14
- 2 Samuel 20:10
- 1 Kings 2:34-35
