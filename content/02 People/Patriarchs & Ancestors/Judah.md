# Judah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Son of Jacob; tribe and later southern kingdom bearing his name
- **Family:** Son of [[Jacob]] and [[Leah]]
- **Key Books:** [[Genesis]], [[Joshua]], [[1 Kings]], [[Nahum]]

## Biography
Judah began as one of Jacob's sons and became the tribe through which kingship and messianic promise would run. After the kingdom divided, Judah also named the southern kingdom centered on [[Jerusalem]].

In [[Nahum]], Judah receives comfort because Assyria's oppressive yoke will be broken. The same name can refer to the patriarch, tribe, land, or kingdom depending on context.

## Key Events
- Jacob's blessing of Judah
- Division of the Kingdom
- Assyrian threat and deliverance
