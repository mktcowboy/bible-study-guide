# Levi

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Son of Jacob; ancestor of the Levitical tribe
- **Family:** Son of [[Jacob]] and [[Leah]]
- **Key Books:** [[Genesis]], [[Exodus]], [[Numbers]], [[Joshua]]

## Biography
Levi was one of the twelve sons of Jacob. His descendants became the tribe set apart for tabernacle service, priestly support, worship, and teaching. Moses, Aaron, Miriam, and the priestly line came from Levi.

## Key Events
- [[Jacob's Ladder]] - Part of Jacob's family line
- [[Tabernacle Consecration]] - Levitical service becomes central to Israel's worship
- [[Levitical Cities]] - Levi's descendants receive cities throughout Israel

## Relationships
- [[Jacob]] - Father
- [[Leah]] - Mother
- [[Moses]] - Descendant
- [[Aaron]] - Descendant and first high priest
- [[Levites]] - Tribal descendants

## Important Verses
- Genesis 29:34 - Leah names Levi.
- Numbers 3:12 - God takes the Levites instead of the firstborn.
- Joshua 21:41 - The Levites receive forty-eight cities.
