# Jonathan

## Summary
Son of [[Saul]], valiant warrior, and covenant friend of [[David]]. Trusted the LORD in battle, defended David against Saul's jealousy, and willingly yielded the future kingdom to the one God had chosen. Died with Saul on [[Mount Gilboa]].

## Key Passages
[[1 Samuel]] 14, 18-20, 23, 31, [[2 Samuel]] 1, 9

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 18:3
- 1 Samuel 20:17
