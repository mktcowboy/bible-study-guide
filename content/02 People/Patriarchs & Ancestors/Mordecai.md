# Mordecai

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Jewish exile in Shushan, guardian of Esther, and later chief official under Ahasuerus
- **Family:** Cousin and adoptive guardian of [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]]
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Mordecai was a Jew of Benjamin living in [[Shushan]] during the reign of [[Ahasuerus]]. He raised Esther after her parents died, instructed her to conceal her Jewish identity, and later uncovered a palace assassination plot by [[Bigthan & Teresh]].

His refusal to bow to [[Haman]] triggered the crisis of the book, yet his faithfulness and Esther's courage became the means of deliverance. After Haman's fall, Mordecai received the signet authority once held by Haman and helped establish [[Purim Established|Purim]] as a perpetual feast.
