# Jethro

## Summary
Priest of Midian. Fathers-in-law of Moses. Gave Zipporah. Gave Moses advice on delegation. Offered sacrifice. Returned to Midian.

## Key Passages
Exodus 2, 18, Deuteronomy 10:19, Judges 1:16
