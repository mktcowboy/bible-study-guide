# Elkanah

## Summary
Ephraimite husband of [[Hannah]] and father of [[Samuel]]. Worshiped yearly at [[Shiloh]] and supported Hannah's vow to dedicate Samuel to the LORD. His household frames the opening scene of 1 Samuel.

## Key Passages
1 Samuel 1-2

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 1:1
- 1 Samuel 1:23

## Related People
[[Hannah]], [[Samuel]], [[Eli]]
