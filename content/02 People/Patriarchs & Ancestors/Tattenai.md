# Tattenai

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Persian governor beyond the River who investigated the resumed temple rebuilding
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]]

## Biography
Tattenai governed the province west of the Euphrates during the reign of Darius I. When [[Zerubbabel]] and [[Jeshua]] resumed work on the temple after prophetic encouragement from [[Haggai (Prophet)|Haggai]] and [[Zechariah (Prophet)|Zechariah]], Tattenai asked who had authorized the rebuilding and sent a formal inquiry to Darius.

His opposition was bureaucratic rather than violent. The investigation led to the discovery of [[Cyrus]]'s original decree, and Darius ordered Tattenai to stay away from the work and fund it from royal revenues.
