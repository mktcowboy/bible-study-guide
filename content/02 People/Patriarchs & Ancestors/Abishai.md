# Abishai

## Summary
Son of Zeruiah, brother of [[Joab]], and one of [[David]]'s fiercest warriors. He repeatedly acts with bold loyalty in battle and crisis, yet his instincts for violent resolution often contrast with David's restraint and political caution.

## Key Passages
[[1 Samuel]] 26, [[2 Samuel]] 2, 10, 16, 18, 19, 21, 23

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 26:8-9
- 2 Samuel 16:9
- 2 Samuel 21:17
