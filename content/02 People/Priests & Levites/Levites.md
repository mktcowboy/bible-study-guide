# Levites

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Tribe set apart for tabernacle and temple service
- **Family:** Descendants of Levi

## Biography
The [[Levites]] descend from Levi, son of Jacob and Leah. After the golden calf episode, the tribe is set apart for service connected to the sanctuary. They do not receive a single tribal territory like the other tribes; instead, they receive cities throughout Israel. In [[1 Chronicles]], they come into sharp focus as singers, gatekeepers, treasurers, and worship servants ordered for temple life.
