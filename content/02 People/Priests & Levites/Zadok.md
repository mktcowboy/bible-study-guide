# Zadok

## Summary
Priest loyal to [[David]] through the upheavals of [[2 Samuel]] and central again in the succession of [[Solomon]]. In [[1 Chronicles]], [[Zadok]] also belongs to the priestly ordering surrounding the ark, worship, and temple preparation.
