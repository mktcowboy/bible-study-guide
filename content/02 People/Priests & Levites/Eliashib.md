# Eliashib

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** High priest during Nehemiah's rebuilding and reforms
- **Family:** Connected to later priestly alliances in Nehemiah
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
Eliashib the high priest helped rebuild the [[Sheep Gate]] with the priests. Later, his alliance with [[Tobiah]] became a serious compromise when a temple chamber was prepared for Tobiah, which [[Nehemiah]] cleared during his reforms.
