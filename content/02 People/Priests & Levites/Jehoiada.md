# Jehoiada

## Summary
Priest who preserves the Davidic heir during the usurpation of [[Athaliah]] and later oversees the public enthronement of [[Joash]]. [[Jehoiada]] stands as a stabilizing priestly figure in one of Judah's most fragile royal moments.
