# Ahimelech

## Summary
Priest at [[Nob]] who gave holy bread and Goliath's sword to [[David]] when David fled from [[Saul]]. Saul later ordered the slaughter of the priests of Nob, making Ahimelech a tragic victim of Saul's paranoia.

## Key Passages
1 Samuel 21-22

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 21:6
- 1 Samuel 22:16

## Related People
[[David]], [[Saul]], [[Doeg the Edomite]]
