# Delilah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Woman in the valley of Sorek who betrays Samson
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Delilah is bribed by Philistine rulers to discover the source of Samson's strength. After repeated pressure, Samson tells her about his Nazirite hair, and she delivers him into Philistine hands.

## Key Events
- [[Samson's Downfall]] — Betrays Samson to the Philistines

## Relationships

## Notes
Delilah's persistence exposes Samson's deeper weakness: not lack of power, but lack of guarded wisdom.
