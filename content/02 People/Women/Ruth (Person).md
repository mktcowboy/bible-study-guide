# Ruth (Person)

## Summary
Moabite widow of [[Mahlon & Chilion|Mahlon]]. Refused to leave [[Naomi]], left [[Moab]] for [[Bethlehem]], gleaned in the fields of [[Boaz]], and became mother of [[Obed]]. Great-grandmother of [[David]] and an ancestor of Christ. Model of covenant loyalty and trusting grace.

## Key Passages
Ruth, Matthew 1:5, Romans 11

## Important Verses
- Ruth 1:16
- Ruth 2:12

## Related People
[[Naomi]], [[Boaz]], [[Obed]], [[David]]
