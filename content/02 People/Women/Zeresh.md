# Zeresh

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Wife of Haman
- **Family:** Wife of [[Haman]]
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Zeresh first advises [[Haman]] to build gallows for [[Mordecai]]. After Haman is forced to honor Mordecai publicly, she interprets his humiliation as the beginning of inevitable defeat if Mordecai is of Jewish descent.
