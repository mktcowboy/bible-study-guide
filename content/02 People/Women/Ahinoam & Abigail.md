# Ahinoam & Abigail

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Wives of David during his fugitive years
- **Family:** Ahinoam of Jezreel; Abigail formerly wife of Nabal
- **Key Books:** [[1 Samuel]], [[2 Samuel]]

## Biography
Ahinoam and Abigail were both wives of David before he became king over all Israel. Abigail is especially known for her wisdom in preventing David from shedding blood against Nabal's household.

## Key Events
- [[David & Nabal-Abigail]] - Abigail intercedes wisely
- [[David's Flight]] - Both women share David's fugitive years

## Relationships
- [[David]] - Husband
- Nabal - Abigail's first husband

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 25:32-33 - David blesses Abigail's wisdom.
- 1 Samuel 30:5 - Ahinoam and Abigail are taken captive at Ziklag.

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[1 Samuel]], [[2 Samuel]]

## Notes
Abigail's wisdom contrasts sharply with Nabal's folly and David's near-vengeance.
