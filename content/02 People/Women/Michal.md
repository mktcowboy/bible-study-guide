# Michal

## Summary
Daughter of [[Saul]] and first wife of [[David]]. She loved David and once saved his life, but her marriage became entangled in political claims between Saul's house and David's. In [[2 Samuel]] she returns to David and later despises his worship before the ark, closing her story in sadness and distance.

## Key Passages
[[1 Samuel]] 18-19, 25:44, [[2 Samuel]] 3:13-16, 6:16-23

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 3:14-16
- 2 Samuel 6:16
- 2 Samuel 6:23
