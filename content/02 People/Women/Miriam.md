# Miriam

## Summary
Sister of Moses and Aaron. Prophetess. Led worship at Red Sea. Rebelled against Moses. Struck with leprosy. Died at Kadesh.

## Key Passages
Exodus 2, 15, Numbers 12, Deuteronomy 24:9, Micah 6:4

## Important Verses
- Exodus 15:20
- Numbers 12:1
