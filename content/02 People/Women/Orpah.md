# Orpah

## Summary
Moabite widow of one of Naomi's sons. Began the return journey with [[Naomi]] but turned back to her people when urged to remain in [[Moab]]. Her departure contrasts with [[Ruth]]'s covenant loyalty.

## Key Passages
Ruth 1:4-15
