# Rahab

## Summary
Harlot of Jericho. Protected Israelite spies. Helped them escape. Spared in conquest. Married Salmon. Mother of Boaz. Ancestor of David and Christ. Example of faith.

## Key Passages
Joshua 2, 6, Matthew 1:5, Hebrews 11:31, James 2:25

## Important Verses
- Joshua 2:1-21
- Hebrews 11:31
