# Rizpah

## Summary
Concubine of [[Saul]] and mother of Armoni and a Mephibosheth distinct from [[Jonathan]]'s son. Her story touches both the struggle around [[Ishbosheth]] in [[2 Samuel]] 3 and the famine tied to Saul's offense against [[The Gibeonites]] in [[2 Samuel]] 21, where her vigil over the executed bodies moves [[David]] to complete the burial of [[Saul]] and [[Jonathan]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 3:7, 21:1-14
