# Lady Folly

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Personification of folly, seduction, and the way to death
- **Family:** Literary personification, not a historical person
- **Key Books:** [[Proverbs]]

## Biography
Lady Folly is the counter-voice to [[Lady Wisdom]]. She is loud, seductive, and spiritually empty, inviting the simple to stolen water and secret bread while hiding that her house leads to death.

The figure draws together Proverbs' warnings about foolishness, sexual temptation, and the allure of shortcuts that reject the fear of the LORD.
