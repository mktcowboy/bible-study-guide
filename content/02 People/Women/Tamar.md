This note focuses on Tamar, the daughter of [[David]] and sister of [[Absalom]] in [[2 Samuel]] 13. Her assault by [[Amnon]] becomes one of the darkest moments in David's house and reveals how the word spoken by [[Nathan]] after the [[Bathsheba]] and [[Uriah the Hittite]] episode unfolds inside the royal family.
