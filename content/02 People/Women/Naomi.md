# Naomi

## Summary
Israelite wife of [[Elimelech]]. Lost husband and sons in [[Moab]], returned empty to [[Bethlehem]], and asked to be called Mara. Through [[Ruth]] and [[Boaz]], the LORD restored her house and carried her story toward the line of [[David]].

## Key Passages
Ruth, Matthew 1:5

## Important Verses
- Ruth 1:20
- Ruth 2:20
