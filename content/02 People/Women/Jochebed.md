# Jochebed

## Summary
Mother of Moses, Aaron, Miriam. Daughter of Levi. Hid Moses three months. Placed him in basket. Faithful mother. Lived 101 years.

## Key Passages
Exodus 2, 6, Numbers 26:59, Hebrews 11:23
