# Bathsheba

## Summary
Wife of [[Uriah the Hittite]] who was taken by [[David]] in sin and later became David's wife after Uriah's death. The child of their first union died under judgment, but she later bore [[Solomon]] and became important in the royal succession. Her story in [[2 Samuel]] is inseparable from both David's failure and God's continuing mercy.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 11-12, [[1 Kings]] 1-2, Matthew 1:6
