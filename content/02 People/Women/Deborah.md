# Deborah

## Summary
Prophetess and judge. Wife of Lapidoth. Called Barak. Defeated Sisera. Song of Deborah. Fourth judge of Israel. Led Israel to victory.

## Key Passages
Judges 4-5, Hebrews 11:32, Psalm 82
