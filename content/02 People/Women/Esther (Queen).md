# Esther (Queen)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Jewish queen of Persia who interceded for her people
- **Family:** Cousin and adopted daughter of [[Mordecai]]
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Esther, also called Hadassah, was a Jewish orphan raised by [[Mordecai]] in [[Shushan]]. After [[Vashti]] was deposed, Esther was brought into the royal selection process and became queen in the court of [[Ahasuerus]].

When [[Haman]] secured a decree to destroy the Jews, Esther risked approaching the king without being summoned. Through fasting, courage, and carefully timed banquets, she exposed Haman's plot and became the instrument of her people's deliverance.
