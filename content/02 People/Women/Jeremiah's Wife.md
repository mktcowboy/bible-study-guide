# Jeremiah's Wife

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Symbolic absence in Jeremiah's sign-act life
- **Family:** Jeremiah was commanded not to marry
- **Key Books:** [[Jeremiah]]

## Biography
Jeremiah 16 records the LORD's command that [[Jeremiah (Prophet)|Jeremiah]] must not take a wife or have children in Judah. The absence of a wife and family became part of his prophetic witness: coming judgment would make ordinary family life a place of grief rather than security.

This note preserves the book-note link while clarifying that Jeremiah's "wife" is not a named person in the narrative but an intentionally forbidden relationship.
