# Abigail

## Summary
Wise and discerning wife of [[Nabal]] who intervened when [[David]] prepared to avenge himself. Her humility and counsel turned David away from needless bloodshed. After Nabal's death she became one of David's wives.

## Key Passages
1 Samuel 25, 2 Samuel 2:2-3

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 25:32-33
- 1 Samuel 25:39

## Related People
[[David]], [[Nabal]]
