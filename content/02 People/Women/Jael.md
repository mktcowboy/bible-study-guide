# Jael

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Woman who kills Sisera
- **Family:** Wife of Heber the Kenite
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Jael receives Sisera into her tent after he flees the battle. While he sleeps, she kills him with a tent peg, fulfilling Deborah's word that the honor of Sisera's death would go to a woman.
