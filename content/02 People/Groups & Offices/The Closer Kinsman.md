# The Closer Kinsman

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Unnamed nearer redeemer in Ruth
- **Family:** Kinsman of Elimelech's family
- **Key Books:** [[Ruth]]

## Biography
The closer kinsman had the first right to redeem Naomi's land, but he declined when he learned redemption included marrying Ruth to raise up the name of the dead. His refusal opened the way for Boaz to redeem.

## Key Events
- [[The Kinsman-Redeemer]] - Declines redemption at Bethlehem's gate

- [[Boaz]] - Next redeemer after him
