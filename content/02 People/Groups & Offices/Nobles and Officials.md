# Nobles and Officials

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Judean leaders confronted by Nehemiah over economic oppression
- **Family:** Civic and social leadership of the returned community
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
The nobles and officials in Nehemiah 5 were lending at interest and taking fields, vineyards, houses, and even children as collateral. [[Nehemiah]] rebuked them publicly and required restitution as part of covenant reform.
