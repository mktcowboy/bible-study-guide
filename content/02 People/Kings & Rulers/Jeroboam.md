# Jeroboam

## Summary
First king of the northern kingdom of Israel. Though raised up through the word of [[Ahijah the Shilonite]], [[Jeroboam]] quickly becomes the model of covenant rebellion by creating rival shrines at [[Bethel]] and [[Laish-Dan|Dan]] in [[Jeroboam's Golden Calves]].
