# Darius I

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Persian king who confirmed Cyrus's decree and supported the second temple rebuilding
- **Family:** Achaemenid ruler; father of [[Ahasuerus]]
- **Key Books:** [[Ezra]], [[Haggai]], [[Zechariah]]

## Biography
Darius I ruled Persia after Cyrus and is central to the completion of the second temple. When [[Tattenai]] investigated the rebuilding, Darius ordered the archives searched, found [[Cyrus]]'s decree, and commanded local officials to support the work.
