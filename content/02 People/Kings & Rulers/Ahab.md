# Ahab

## Summary
King of Israel and son of Omri who becomes the darkest royal example in [[1 Kings]]. Under the influence of [[Jezebel]], [[Ahab]] promotes Baal worship, opposes [[Elijah]], covets [[Naboth]]'s inheritance, and dies under prophetic judgment after rejecting [[Micaiah son of Imlah]].
