# Jotham

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Surviving son of Gideon who condemns Abimelech
- **Family:** Son of [[Gideon]]
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Jotham escapes Abimelech's massacre of Gideon's sons. From Mount Gerizim he speaks the parable of the trees, warning Shechem that Abimelech's rule will consume both ruler and supporters.

## Key Events
- [[Abimelech's Usurpation]] — Jotham survives and announces judgment

## Relationships
- [[Abimelech]] — Half-brother and murderer of Gideon's sons
- [[Gideon]] — Father

## Important Verses
> "Hearken unto me, ye men of Shechem, that God may hearken unto you." — Judges 9:7

## Notes
