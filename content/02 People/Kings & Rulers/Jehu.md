# Jehu

## Summary
Military commander anointed to destroy the house of [[Ahab]]. [[Jehu]] executes sweeping judgment in Israel, including the deaths of [[Jezebel]] and Ahab's sons, yet he does not depart from the sins of [[Jeroboam]].
