# Eglon

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** King of Moab who oppresses Israel
- **Family:** Moabite ruler
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Eglon gathers Moab, Ammon, and Amalek against Israel and rules over Israel for eighteen years. He is assassinated by [[Ehud]], after which Israel defeats Moab.

## Key Events
- [[Ehud's Assassination]] — Eglon is killed by Ehud

## Relationships
- [[Ehud]] — Judge who kills him

## Important Verses
> "So the children of Israel served Eglon the king of Moab eighteen years." — Judges 3:14

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Judges]]

## Notes
Eglon's downfall is narrated with deliberate irony, highlighting the reversal of oppressive power.
