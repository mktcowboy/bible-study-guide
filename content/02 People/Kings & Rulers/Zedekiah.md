# Zedekiah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Last king of Judah before Jerusalem fell to Babylon
- **Family:** Son of Josiah; uncle of Jehoiachin
- **Key Books:** [[2 Kings]], [[Jeremiah]], [[2 Chronicles]]

## Biography
Zedekiah was installed by [[Nebuchadnezzar]] as Judah's final king. Though [[Jeremiah]] repeatedly urged him to submit to Babylon as God's judgment, Zedekiah wavered between fear of officials, secret interest in Jeremiah's word, and political rebellion.

When Jerusalem fell, Zedekiah fled but was captured near Jericho. At Riblah he saw his sons killed, then his eyes were put out and he was taken in chains to Babylon.
