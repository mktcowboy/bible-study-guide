# Hezekiah

## Summary
King of Judah whose reign becomes one of the brightest moments in the late monarchy. [[Hezekiah]] removes idolatrous practices, trusts God during the Assyrian crisis, and receives an extended life after prayer, though Babylonian shadows already gather at the end of his story.
