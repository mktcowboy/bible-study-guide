# Nebuchadnezzar

## Summary
Babylonian king who subdues Judah, carries its people into exile, and oversees the destruction of [[Jerusalem]] and the temple. In [[2 Kings]], [[Nebuchadnezzar]] becomes the human face of Judah's covenant judgment.
