# Adonijah

## Summary
Fourth son of [[David]] who attempted to seize the throne near the end of David's life. His self-exaltation in [[Solomon's Succession]] failed when [[Nathan]], [[Bathsheba]], [[Zadok]], and [[Benaiah]] secured [[Solomon]]'s anointing at [[Gihon]].
