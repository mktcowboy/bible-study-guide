# Jabin

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Kingly name associated with Hazor
- **Family:** Canaanite royal line
- **Key Books:** [[Joshua]], [[Judges]]

## Biography
Jabin was the king of [[Hazor]] who gathered the northern coalition against Israel in Joshua 11. Joshua defeated him and burned Hazor. A later Jabin, also king of Hazor, oppressed Israel in Judges 4 until the LORD delivered Israel through Deborah and Barak.

## Key Events
- [[Northern Campaign]] - Joshua defeated Jabin's coalition
- Deborah and Barak - A later Jabin oppressed Israel

## Relationships
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] - Defeated Jabin in the conquest
- [[Deborah]] and Barak - Connected to the later Hazor oppression

## Important Verses
- Joshua 11:1 - Jabin king of Hazor gathered northern kings.
- Joshua 11:10 - Hazor was the head of those kingdoms.
- Judges 4:2 - A later Jabin oppressed Israel.

## Cross-References
- Mentioned in: [[Joshua]], [[Judges]]

## Notes
The repeated name Jabin links Joshua's conquest with Judges' unfinished struggle against Canaanite power.
