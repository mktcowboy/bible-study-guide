# Abimelech

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Son of Gideon who makes himself king in Shechem
- **Family:** Son of [[Gideon]] by a concubine from [[Shechem]]
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Abimelech persuades Shechem to support him, murders his brothers, and rules violently. Jotham's parable of the trees condemns him, and his reign ends when a woman drops a millstone on his head at Thebez.

## Key Events
- [[Abimelech's Usurpation]] — Murders Gideon's sons and rules over Shechem

## Relationships
- [[Gideon]] — Father
- [[Jotham]] — Surviving brother who pronounces judgment

## Important Verses

## Cross-References

## Notes
