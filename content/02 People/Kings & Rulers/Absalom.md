# Absalom

## Summary
Son of [[David]] known for extraordinary beauty, deep resentment, and political ambition. After avenging [[Tamar]] by killing [[Amnon]], he slowly positioned himself against David, led a major rebellion, and died in defeat through the hand of [[Joab]]. His story is one of the central tragedies of [[2 Samuel]].

[[2 Samuel]] 13-19, 1 Chronicles 3:1-3
