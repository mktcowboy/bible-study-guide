# Ishbosheth

## Summary
Son of [[Saul]] who was installed by [[Abner]] as king over Israel after Saul's death while [[David]] ruled Judah from Hebron. His weak and troubled reign in [[2 Samuel]] exposes the collapse of Saul's house, and his murder helps clear the way for David's united monarchy.
