# Jehoshaphat

## Summary
King of Judah who appears late in [[1 Kings]] as a comparatively faithful ruler but enters a dangerous alliance with [[Ahab]]. His presence in the [[Micaiah and Ahab's Death]] narrative highlights the tension between covenant faithfulness and political compromise.
