# Sennacherib

## Summary
Assyrian king who invades Judah during the reign of [[Hezekiah]] and arrogantly defies the living God. His failed siege of [[Jerusalem]] becomes one of the clearest demonstrations of divine deliverance in [[2 Kings]].
