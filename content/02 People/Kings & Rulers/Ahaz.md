# Ahaz

## Summary
King of Judah whose fear and compromise deepen Judah's decline before the reign of [[Hezekiah]]. Though more central in [[Isaiah]], [[Ahaz]] forms part of the darker background against which Hezekiah's trust stands out in [[2 Kings]].
