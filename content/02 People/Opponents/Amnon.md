# Amnon

## Summary
Firstborn son of [[David]]. His violence against his sister [[Tamar]] shatters David's household and sets in motion [[Absalom]]'s revenge, advancing the judgment announced by [[Nathan]] after the sin involving [[Bathsheba]] and [[Uriah the Hittite]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 3:2, 13

## Important Verses

- 2 Samuel 13:15
- 2 Samuel 13:28-29
