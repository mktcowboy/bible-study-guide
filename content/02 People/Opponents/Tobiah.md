# Tobiah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Ammonite official and opponent of Nehemiah
- **Family:** Connected by marriage to influential Judean families
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
Tobiah joined [[Sanballat]] in opposing [[Nehemiah]] from the moment the rebuilding mission began. He mocked the wall as too weak to stand, exchanged intimidating letters with Judean nobles, and later occupied a chamber in the temple precincts through his alliance with Eliashib.

Nehemiah's final reforms include throwing Tobiah's household goods out of the temple chamber, a vivid sign that covenant renewal required removing compromised loyalties from the house of God.
