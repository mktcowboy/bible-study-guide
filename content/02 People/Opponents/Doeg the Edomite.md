# Doeg the Edomite

## Summary
Servant of [[Saul]] who informed on [[Ahimelech]] and carried out Saul's order to kill the priests at [[Nob]]. His violence becomes a biblical picture of treachery and malicious speech.

## Key Passages
1 Samuel 21-22, Psalm 52

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 22:18
- Psalm 52:2

## Related People
[[Saul]], [[Ahimelech]], [[David]]
