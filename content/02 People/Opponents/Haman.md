# Haman

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Agagite official who plotted the destruction of the Jews
- **Family:** Husband of Zeresh; called son of Hammedatha
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Haman rose to high office under [[Ahasuerus]] and demanded public honor. When [[Mordecai]] refused to bow, Haman's rage expanded into a genocidal plot against all Jews in the Persian empire.

His story is marked by reversal. He built gallows for Mordecai, was forced to honor Mordecai publicly, and was finally hanged on the gallows he had prepared.
