# Sisera

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Commander of Jabin's army
- **Family:** Associated with Harosheth of the Gentiles
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Sisera commands nine hundred iron chariots and oppresses Israel for twenty years. After the LORD routs his army before Barak, Sisera flees on foot and is killed by Jael.

## Key Events
- [[Deborah and Barak]] — Sisera's army is defeated

## Relationships
- [[Barak]] — Pursues him
- [[Deborah]] — Prophesies his defeat
- [[Jael]] — Kills him
