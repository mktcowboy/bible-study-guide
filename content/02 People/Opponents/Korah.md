# Korah

## Summary
Levite who rebelled against Moses authority with Dathan and Abiram. Earth swallowed them. Warning against rebellion. Sons of Kohath.

## Key Passages
Numbers 16, Psalm 106:16-18, 1 Corinthians 10:10, 2 Peter 2:6, Jude 1:11
