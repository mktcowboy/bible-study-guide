# The Offender

## Overview
The offender in [[2 Corinthians]] is the disciplined person whom Paul urges the church to forgive and restore.

## Biblical Significance
The note likely refers to a member of the Corinthian church whose sin and discipline had grieved the congregation. Paul's concern is that punishment should give way to comfort when repentance is evident.
