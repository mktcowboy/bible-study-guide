# Shimei

## Summary
Benjamite from [[Bahurim]] who cursed [[David]] during [[Absalom's Rebellion]] and later begged for mercy when David returned. He represents the bitterness of Saul's old loyalties and the unstable political mood surrounding David's restoration.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 16:5-13, 19:16-23, [[1 Kings]] 2:8-9, 36-46

## Important Verses
- 2 Samuel 16:7-8
- 2 Samuel 19:18-23

## Related People
[[David]], [[Abishai]], [[Absalom]]
