# Sanballat

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Horonite opponent of Nehemiah's wall rebuilding
- **Family:** Father-in-law connection to the high priestly family through Joiada's son
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
Sanballat was one of the chief external opponents of [[Nehemiah]]. He mocked the rebuilding effort, conspired with regional allies, tried to lure Nehemiah away from the work, and hired prophetic intimidation to make him afraid.

His resistance was political and spiritual: the rebuilt wall meant Jerusalem's renewed stability, and Nehemiah's reforms threatened the compromised alliances that had grown during exile and return.
