# Geshem

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Arabian leader allied against Nehemiah
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Nehemiah]]

## Biography
Geshem appears as part of the regional coalition resisting Jerusalem's restoration. Alongside [[Sanballat]] and [[Tobiah]], he mocked Nehemiah's mission and later helped send repeated invitations meant to draw Nehemiah away from the wall to the plain of Ono.

His role is brief but important: he represents the wider political pressure surrounding Jerusalem as the returned community rebuilt its defenses.
