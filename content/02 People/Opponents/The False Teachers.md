# The False Teachers

## Overview
The false teachers in [[Jude]] are ungodly infiltrators who distort grace, deny the Lord, and lead others toward judgment.

## Biblical Significance
Jude portrays them through biblical examples and vivid metaphors: Cain, Balaam, Korah, waterless clouds, dead trees, and wandering stars.

## Related Notes
- [[Jude]]
- [[2 Peter]]
- [[Balaam]]
- [[Korah]]

## Notes
Jude's warning is severe because false teaching is treated as both doctrinal corruption and moral rebellion.
