# Naboth

## Summary
Jezreelite landowner who refused to surrender his inherited vineyard to [[Ahab]]. His faithful refusal and unjust death become the center of [[Naboth's Vineyard]] and one of the clearest indictments of royal abuse in the Old Testament.
