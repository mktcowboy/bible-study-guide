# Sheba

## Summary
Benjaminite rebel, the son of Bichri, who exploits the instability after [[Absalom]]'s revolt to challenge [[David]]'s rule. His uprising in [[2 Samuel]] 20 exposes how fragile the kingdom remains, and it ends only after [[Joab]] kills [[Amasa]] and Sheba is delivered from Abel.
