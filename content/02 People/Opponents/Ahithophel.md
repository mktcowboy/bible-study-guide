# Ahithophel

## Summary
Brilliant counselor to [[David]] whose advice carried near-oracular weight, yet he defects to [[Absalom]] during the rebellion. His contest with [[Hushai]] becomes a strategic hinge in [[2 Samuel]] 15-17, and his suicide marks the unraveling of Absalom's cause.

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 15-17
