# Alexander the Coppersmith

## Overview
Alexander the coppersmith is named in [[2 Timothy]] as someone who did Paul much harm.

## Biblical Significance
Paul warns Timothy about Alexander while entrusting final justice to the Lord. The note is distinct from the Alexander named in [[1 Timothy]] because the text does not explicitly identify them as the same person.

## Key People Associated
- [[Paul (Apostle)]]
- [[Timothy]]

## Related Notes
- [[2 Timothy]]

## Notes
Alexander's opposition belongs to Paul's final warnings about endurance and faithfulness.
