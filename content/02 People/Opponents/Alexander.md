# Alexander

## Overview
Alexander is named with Hymenaeus in [[1 Timothy]] as one who made shipwreck of the faith and was delivered to Satan for discipline.

## Biblical Significance
He represents the danger of rejecting faith and a good conscience within the church's life.

## Key People Associated
- [[Paul (Apostle)]]
- [[Timothy]]

## Related Notes
- [[1 Timothy]]

## Notes
This Alexander may or may not be the same person as Alexander the coppersmith in [[2 Timothy]], so the vault keeps the notes distinct.
