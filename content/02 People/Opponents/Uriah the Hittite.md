# Uriah the Hittite

## Summary
Faithful husband of [[Bathsheba]] and one of [[David]]'s mighty men. His integrity during wartime exposes David's sin, and his arranged death becomes the turning point that brings [[Nathan]]'s rebuke and lasting judgment on David's house in [[2 Samuel]].

## Key Passages
[[2 Samuel]] 11-12, 23:39, [[Matthew]] 1:6

## Important Verses

- 2 Samuel 11:17
- 2 Samuel 12:9
