# Bigthan & Teresh

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Palace officials who plotted against King Ahasuerus
- **Family:** Not specified
- **Key Books:** [[Esther]]

## Biography
Bigthan and Teresh were two of the king's chamberlains who guarded the threshold. They became angry with [[Ahasuerus]] and planned to lay hands on him, but [[Mordecai]] learned of the plot and reported it through [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]].

The plot was investigated, confirmed, and the conspirators were hanged. Mordecai's service was recorded but initially unrewarded, setting up the sleepless-night reversal of Esther 6.
