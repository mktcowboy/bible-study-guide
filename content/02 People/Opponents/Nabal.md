# Nabal

## Summary
Wealthy but foolish man of Carmel who insulted [[David]] despite David's protection of his shepherds. His hardness nearly provoked David to bloodshed, but [[Abigail]] intervened. Nabal later died under the LORD's judgment.

1 Samuel 25
