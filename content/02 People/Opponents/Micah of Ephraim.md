# Micah of Ephraim

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Role:** Ephraimite who creates a private shrine
- **Family:** Son of an unnamed mother
- **Key Books:** [[Judges]]

## Biography
Micah steals silver from his mother, returns it, and uses part of it to make an idol. He installs one of his sons as priest, then hires a wandering Levite. His shrine is later stolen by the tribe of Dan.

## Key Events
- [[Micah's Idolatry]] — Private shrine reveals religious disorder
- [[Danite Migration]] — Danites steal Micah's idol and priest

## Relationships
- [[Levites]] — A Levite serves at his shrine
