# Barnabas

## Summary
Son of encouragement. Sold land, gave to apostles. Took Paul to Jerusalem. Went with Paul on first journey. Parted with Paul over Mark. Went to Cyprus with Mark. Prophet.

## Key Passages
Acts 4, 9, 11, 13, 15, Galatians 2, Colossians 4:10, 2 Timothy 4:10, 1 Peter 1:13

## Important Verses
- Acts 4:36-37
- Acts 9:27
