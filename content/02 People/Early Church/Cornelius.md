# Cornelius

## Summary
Centurion. Italian band. God-fearing. First Gentile convert. Angel sent to Joppa. Peters sermon. Holy Spirit fell. Baptized. Opened door to Gentiles.

## Key Passages
Acts 10-11, Romans 11:13, Galatians 2:7-8
