# Titus (Companion of Paul)

## Summary
Greek. Companion of Paul. Went to Jerusalem council. Left in Crete. Went to Macedonia. Comforted Paul. Co-sender of 2 Corinthians. Deacon.

## Key Passages
Acts 15, 2 Corinthians, Galatians 2, Titus, 2 Timothy 4:10
