# Onesimus

## Summary
Fugitive slave of Philemon. Converted by Paul. Sent back with letter. No longer a slave, but brother. Probably later bishop of Ephesus.

## Key Passages
Philemon, Colossians 4:9

## Important Verses
- Philemon 1:10-11
