# James (Brother of Lord)

## Summary
Half-brother of Jesus. Did not believe initially. Leader of Jerusalem church. Wrote epistle. Martyred. Pillar of church. Practical wisdom.

## Key Passages
Matthew 13:55, Mark 6:3, Galatians 1:19, 2:9, James, Acts 12:17, 15, 21

## Important Verses
- James 1:2
- James 2:26
