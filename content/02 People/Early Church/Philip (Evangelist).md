# Philip (Evangelist)

## Summary
One of seven deacons. Hellenist. Preached in Samaria. Ethiopian eunuch. Daughters prophesied. Four daughters. Apostle to Samaria and Ethiopia.

## Key Passages
Acts 6, 8, Philippians 1:1
