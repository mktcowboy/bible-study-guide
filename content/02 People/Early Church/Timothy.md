# Timothy

## Summary
Young pastor. Mother Lois, mother Eunice. Circumcised. Companion of Paul. Left at Ephesus. Suffered. Preach the word. Fought good fight. Beloved son.

## Key Passages
Acts 16, 1 & 2 Timothy, Romans 16:21, Philippians 1:1, Colossians 1:1, Philemon 1:1, 1 & 2 Thessalonians
