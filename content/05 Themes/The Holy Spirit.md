# The Holy Spirit

## Overview
The Holy Spirit is the divine presence and power of God active in creation, prophecy, renewal, mission, sanctification, and resurrection life.

## Canonical Development
In the Old Testament, the Spirit empowers creation, craftsmen, judges, kings, and prophets. The prophets promise a deeper future work: the Spirit poured out broadly, giving new hearts and covenant obedience. In the New Testament, that promise comes into public fullness at Pentecost, where the risen Christ pours out the Spirit on the church.
