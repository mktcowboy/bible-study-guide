# The Word

## Overview
The Word is John's title for the eternal Son who was with God, was God, and became flesh in [[Jesus Christ]].

## Biblical Significance
[[John]] opens by echoing [[Genesis]]: the God who creates by speaking now reveals himself personally in the incarnate Word.

## Notes
The Word language holds together divine revelation, creation, incarnation, and life.
