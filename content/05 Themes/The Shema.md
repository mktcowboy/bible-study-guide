# The Shema

## Overview
The Shema is Israel's central confession from [[Deuteronomy]] 6:4-5: the Lord is one, and Israel must love him with the whole heart, soul, and might.

## Biblical Significance
Moses placed the Shema at the heart of covenant life. It binds exclusive worship to whole-person love and becomes a daily confession of Israel's identity before God.

## Key Passages
- Deuteronomy 6:4-5
- Deuteronomy 11:13-21
- Mark 12:29-31
