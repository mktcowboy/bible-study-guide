# Rich and Poor

## Overview
Rich and poor is a major biblical theme tracing God's concern for the vulnerable, his warning to the powerful, and the reversal of status in the kingdom.

## Biblical Significance
In [[James]], wealth becomes a test of community faithfulness. The poor are not to be dishonored, the rich are not to be flattered, and those who oppress laborers are warned that the Lord hears the cries of the defrauded.

## Key Passages
- James 1:9-11
- James 2:1-13
- James 5:1-6
- Luke 1:52-53
- Luke 6:20-26

## Notes
The Bible's concern is not envy of wealth but covenant justice: resources, honor, and power must be handled before God on behalf of neighbor.
