# The Son of Man

## Overview
The Son of Man is the heavenly figure in [[Daniel]] 7 who comes with the clouds, approaches the Ancient of Days, and receives everlasting dominion over all peoples and nations. The title becomes Jesus' primary self-designation in the Gospels.

## Biblical Development
In Daniel, "one like a son of man" contrasts with the beastly empires: human-like, heaven-associated, and given a kingdom that will not pass away. In Ezekiel, "son of man" also names prophetic human frailty before divine majesty, giving the phrase both humility and exaltation.

Jesus joins these strands when he calls himself the Son of Man: he suffers, serves, forgives sins, and will come on the clouds in glory.
