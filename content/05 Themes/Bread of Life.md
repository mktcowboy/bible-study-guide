# Bread of Life

## Overview
Bread of Life is one of Jesus' "I AM" sayings in [[John]] 6.

## Biblical Significance
After feeding the crowd, Jesus identifies himself as the true bread from heaven who gives life to the world.

## Notes
The Bread of Life discourse connects wilderness provision, faith, and the life Jesus gives.
