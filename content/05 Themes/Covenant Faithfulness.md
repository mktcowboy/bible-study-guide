# Covenant Faithfulness

## Overview
Covenant faithfulness is God's steadfast love, loyalty, and mercy toward the people to whom he has bound himself by promise.

## Canonical Development
Scripture repeatedly shows God's people failing their obligations while God remains faithful to his name, oath, and redemptive purpose. This steadfast love undergirds the covenant story from Abraham to the New Covenant and reaches its climax in Christ.

## Notes
Hesed is not generic kindness. It is loyal love in covenant relationship: mercy with memory, affection with commitment, grace that keeps its promises.
