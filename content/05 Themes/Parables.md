# Parables

## Overview
Parables are short figurative stories used by [[Jesus Christ]] to reveal the kingdom of God while also exposing the listener's heart.

## Biblical Significance
The Gospels use parables to teach mercy, judgment, repentance, discipleship, and the hidden-yet-growing reign of God.
