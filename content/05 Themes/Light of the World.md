# Light of the World

## Overview
Light of the World is one of Jesus' "I AM" sayings in [[John]].

## Biblical Significance
Jesus presents himself as the light that exposes darkness, guides his people, and gives the light of life.

## Notes
John's light theme begins in the prologue and runs through conflict, witness, and belief.
