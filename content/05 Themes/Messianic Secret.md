# Messianic Secret

## Overview
The Messianic Secret is the pattern in [[Mark]] where Jesus commands demons, healed people, and disciples not to publicize his identity too early.

## Biblical Significance
Mark uses this pattern to show that Jesus' identity cannot be rightly understood apart from the cross.

## Related Notes
- [[Mark]]
- [[Peter's Confession]]
- [[Transfiguration]]
- [[Crucifixion]]

## Notes
The secret is not denial of messiahship; it is control of misunderstanding until suffering reveals the Messiah's mission.
