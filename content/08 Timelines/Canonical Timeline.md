# Canonical Timeline

## Primeval History
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Creation | [[Creation]] | God creates the heavens, earth, humanity, and Sabbath rest. |
| Fall | [[Fall of Man]] | Sin and death enter the human story. |
| Flood | [[The Flood]] | Judgment and preservation through Noah. |
| Babel | [[Tower of Babel]] | Human pride scattered into nations and languages. |

## Patriarchs
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Abraham | [[Call of Abraham]], [[Abrahamic Covenant]] | God calls Abraham and promises land, seed, and blessing. |
| Isaac | [[Birth of Isaac]] | The promised son continues the covenant line. |
| Jacob | [[Jacob's Ladder]], [[Jacob Wrestles with God]] | Jacob becomes Israel and fathers the tribes. |
| Joseph | [[Joseph Sold into Slavery]], [[Joseph in Egypt]] | God preserves the family through famine in Egypt. |

## Exodus and Wilderness
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Deliverance | [[Passover]], [[Red Sea Crossing]] | Israel is redeemed from Egypt. |
| Sinai | [[Ten Commandments Given]], [[Tabernacle Constructed]] | Covenant law and sanctuary pattern are given. |
| Wilderness | [[Spy Mission and Rebellion]], [[Death of Moses]] | The first generation falls in the wilderness; Moses dies outside the land. |

## Land, Kingdom, and Exile
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Conquest | [[Fall of Jericho]] | Joshua leads Israel into Canaan. |
| Judges | [[Judges]] | Cycles of apostasy, oppression, deliverance, and relapse. |
| United monarchy | [[Davidic Covenant]], [[Temple Construction]], [[Temple Dedication]] | David receives the kingdom promise; Solomon builds the temple. |
| Divided kingdom | [[1 Kings]], [[2 Kings]] | Israel and Judah divide and decline. |
| Assyrian crisis | [[Fall of Samaria]] | The northern kingdom falls to Assyria. |
| Babylonian exile | [[Fall of Jerusalem]] | Jerusalem and the temple fall to Babylon. |

## Return and Second Temple
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Return | [[First Return]], [[Altar Rebuilt]] | Exiles return under Persian rule. |
| Rebuilding | [[Temple Completed]], [[Ezra]], [[Nehemiah]] | Temple, Torah, walls, and community life are restored. |
| Preservation | [[Esther]] | Jewish survival in Persia is remembered through Purim. |

## Christ and the Church
| Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-----|--------------|---------|
| Incarnation | [[Birth of Jesus]] | The promised Messiah enters Israel's story. |
| Public ministry | [[Baptism of Jesus]], [[Temptation of Jesus]] | Jesus is revealed, tested, and begins kingdom ministry. |
| Passion and resurrection | [[Crucifixion]], [[Resurrection]] | Christ dies for sins and rises from the dead. |
| Church mission | [[Pentecost and the Birth of the Church]], [[Conversion of Saul]], [[Jerusalem Council]], [[Paul in Rome]] | The gospel moves from Jerusalem toward the nations. |
| Consummation | [[New Jerusalem]] | The canon ends with judgment, victory, and new creation. |

## Notes
The canonical story moves from creation to covenant, from kingdom to exile, from Christ to church, and from resurrection to new creation.
