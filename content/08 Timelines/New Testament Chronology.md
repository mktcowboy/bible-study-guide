# New Testament Chronology

## Gospel Era
| Approx. Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-------------|--------------|---------|
| Birth and childhood | [[Birth of Jesus]], [[Presentation of Jesus]], [[Boy Jesus in the Temple]] | Jesus is born, presented in the temple, and later appears there at age twelve. |
| Beginning of ministry | [[Baptism of Jesus]], [[Temptation of Jesus]] | Jesus is baptized, tested, and begins public ministry. |
| Galilean and Judean ministry | [[Sermon on the Mount]], [[Miracles]], [[Water to Wine]], [[Samaritan Woman]], [[Lazarus Raised]] | Jesus teaches, heals, reveals his identity, and calls for faith. |
| Passion and resurrection | [[Passion Week]], [[Last Supper]], [[Crucifixion]], [[Resurrection]] | Jesus is betrayed, crucified, buried, and raised. |

## Acts Era
| Approx. Era | Anchor Notes | Summary |
|-------------|--------------|---------|
| Jerusalem church | [[Pentecost and the Birth of the Church]], [[Stephen's Martyrdom]] | The Spirit is given and persecution scatters witness. |
| Gentile inclusion | [[Cornelius's Vision]], [[Jerusalem Council]] | Gentiles are received without becoming Jews. |
| Paul's mission | [[Conversion of Saul]], [[Paul's First Missionary Journey]], [[Paul's Second Missionary Journey]], [[Paul's Third Missionary Journey]] | Paul carries the gospel through the eastern Mediterranean. |
| Rome | [[Paul in Rome]] | Acts ends with Paul preaching in Rome under guard. |

## Epistles and Revelation
| Cluster | Books | Orientation |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Early general instruction | [[James]] | Practical wisdom for scattered believers. |
| Pauline mission letters | [[Galatians]], [[1 Thessalonians]], [[2 Thessalonians]], [[1 Corinthians]], [[2 Corinthians]], [[Romans]] | Gospel, churches, justification, resurrection, and hope. |
| Prison and pastoral letters | [[Ephesians]], [[Philippians]], [[Colossians]], [[Philemon]], [[1 Timothy]], [[2 Timothy]], [[Titus]] | Church, Christ's supremacy, pastoral order, endurance. |
| General epistles and apocalypse | [[Hebrews]], [[1 Peter]], [[2 Peter]], [[1 John]], [[2 John]], [[3 John]], [[Jude]], [[Revelation]] | Perseverance, truth, false teaching, suffering, and final victory. |

## Notes
The New Testament moves from the ministry of Christ to the mission of the Spirit-empowered church, then to epistolary instruction for communities awaiting Christ's return.
