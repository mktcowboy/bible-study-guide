## Old Testament

### Pentateuch
- [[Genesis]] - creation, fall, patriarchs, Joseph
- [[Exodus]] - deliverance, Passover, Sinai, tabernacle
- [[Leviticus]] - sacrifice, priesthood, holiness, feasts
- [[Numbers]] - wilderness rebellion, Balaam, new generation
- [[Deuteronomy]] - Moses' farewell, law restated, covenant renewal

### Historical Books
- [[Joshua]] - conquest, land division, covenant renewal
- [[Judges]] - apostasy, deliverance, collapse
- [[Ruth]] - loyalty, redemption, Davidic line
- [[1 Samuel]] - Samuel, Saul, David's rise
- [[2 Samuel]] - David's reign, covenant, family crisis
- [[1 Kings]] - Solomon, temple, divided kingdom, Elijah
