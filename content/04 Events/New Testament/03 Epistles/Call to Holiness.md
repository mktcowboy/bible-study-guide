# Call to Holiness

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[1 Peter]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Peter 1:13-21
- **Summary:** Peter calls believers to prepare their minds, hope fully in grace, and live as holy children before their Father.

## Details
Peter grounds Christian holiness in new identity and costly redemption. Because God is holy, his people must refuse their former ignorant passions and live in reverent fear, remembering that they were ransomed not with silver or gold but with the precious blood of Christ.

## Key People Involved
- [[Peter]] - Author and apostolic exhorter
- [[Jesus Christ]] - The spotless lamb whose blood redeems
