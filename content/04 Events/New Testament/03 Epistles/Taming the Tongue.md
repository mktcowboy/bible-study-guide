# Taming the Tongue

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[James]]
- **Chapters:** James 3:1-12
- **Summary:** James warns that the tongue, though small, can steer, ignite, corrupt, bless, and curse.

## Details
James piles up images for speech: a bit in a horse's mouth, a rudder on a ship, a spark that sets a forest ablaze, a restless evil full of deadly poison. The scandal is that the same mouth can bless God and curse people made in God's likeness.
