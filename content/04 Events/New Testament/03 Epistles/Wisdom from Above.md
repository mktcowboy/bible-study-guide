# Wisdom from Above

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[James]]
- **Chapters:** James 3:13-18
- **Summary:** James contrasts earthly wisdom marked by envy with heavenly wisdom marked by purity, peace, mercy, and good fruit.

## Details
True wisdom is visible in conduct. James rejects bitter envy and selfish ambition as earthly, unspiritual, and demonic. Wisdom from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, open to reason, full of mercy and good fruits, impartial, and sincere.
