# Living Stones and Holy Priesthood

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[1 Peter]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Peter 2:4-10
- **Summary:** Peter describes believers as living stones built into a spiritual house and as a royal priesthood.

## Details
Coming to Christ, the living stone rejected by men and chosen by God, believers become living stones themselves. Peter applies Israel's covenant language to the church: chosen generation, royal priesthood, holy nation, and God's own people called out of darkness into marvelous light.

## Key People Involved
- [[Peter]] - Author
- [[Jesus Christ]] - Cornerstone and living stone

## Notes
