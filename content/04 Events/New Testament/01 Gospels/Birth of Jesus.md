# Birth of Jesus

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[Matthew]], [[Luke]]
- **Chapters:** Matthew 1-2; Luke 1-2
- **Summary:** [[Jesus Christ]] is born in [[Bethlehem]], fulfilling the Davidic and messianic promises.

## Details
Jesus is conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of Mary, and publicly identified as the promised son of David. Matthew emphasizes fulfillment and royal threat; Luke emphasizes humble arrival, angelic announcement, and joy.

## Key People Involved
- [[Jesus Christ]] — Messiah born in Bethlehem
- [[Mary (Mother of Jesus)]] — Mother of Jesus

## Key Places
- [[Bethlehem]] — Birthplace of Jesus

## Important Verses
> "For unto you is born this day in the city of David a Saviour, which is Christ the Lord." — Luke 2:11
