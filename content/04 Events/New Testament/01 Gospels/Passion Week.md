# Passion Week

## Overview
Passion Week is the final week of Jesus' earthly ministry, moving from his entry into Jerusalem to the cross and empty tomb.

## Biblical Significance
In [[Mark]], nearly half the Gospel is devoted to this week, showing that the cross is not an accident but the mission's climax.
