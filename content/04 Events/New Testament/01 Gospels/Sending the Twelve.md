# Sending the Twelve

## Overview
Jesus commissioned [[The Twelve]] to proclaim the kingdom, heal the sick, and prepare Israel for his mission.

## Biblical Significance
The sending in [[Matthew]] 10 forms the Mission Discourse, joining authority, dependence, persecution, and faithful witness.

## Key People Involved
- [[Jesus Christ]]
- [[The Twelve]]

## Related Notes
- [[Matthew]]
- [[Mark]]
- [[Luke]]

## Notes
The mission of the Twelve previews the wider mission that will expand after the resurrection.
