# Miracles

## Overview
The miracles of [[Jesus Christ]] reveal his authority over disease, demons, nature, death, and sin.

## Biblical Significance
In the Gospels, miracles are not spectacle; they are signs of the kingdom and embodied mercy for the afflicted.

## Notes
