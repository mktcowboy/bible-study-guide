# The Seven I Am Statements

## Overview
- **Testament:** New
- **Book:** [[John]]
- **Chapters:** John 6:35; 8:12; 10:7,9; 10:11,14; 11:25; 14:6; 15:1,5
- **Summary:** Distributed across the second half of John's Gospel, seven distinct "I AM" declarations of Jesus form one of the book's primary literary and theological structures. Each declaration pairs the divine name with a metaphor that illuminates an aspect of Christ's saving work. Together they constitute the most concentrated self-revelation of Jesus found in any single Gospel.

## Significance
The Greek phrase *egō eimi* ("I am") deliberately echoes the Septuagint's rendering of the divine name in Exodus 3:14, where God reveals himself to Moses as "I AM WHO I AM" (*egō eimi ho ōn*). John's audience, familiar with the Greek OT, would hear in each declaration a claim to divine identity, not merely descriptive self-introduction. The absolute use of *egō eimi* in John 8:58 — "Before Abraham was, I am" — makes this identification explicit and provoked a charge of blasphemy. Each metaphorical "I AM" thus functions as both revelatory image and implicit divine claim.

## The Seven Declarations

| # | Statement | Reference | Old Testament Echo |
|---|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| 1 | "I am the bread of life" | John 6:35 | Manna in the wilderness (Exodus 16) |
| 2 | "I am the light of the world" | John 8:12 | Pillar of fire; Psalm 27:1; Isaiah 9:2 |
| 3 | "I am the door of the sheep" | John 10:7,9 | God as gate and guardian of Israel |
| 4 | "I am the good shepherd" | John 10:11,14 | Psalm 23; Ezekiel 34 — the LORD as shepherd |
| 5 | "I am the resurrection and the life" | John 11:25 | Daniel 12:2 — resurrection of the dead |
| 6 | "I am the way, the truth, and the life" | John 14:6 | Exodus path through the wilderness |
| 7 | "I am the true vine" | John 15:1,5 | Israel as the vine (Psalm 80:8; Isaiah 5:1-7) |

## Key Verses
> "Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Before Abraham was, I am." — John 8:58 (KJV)

> "Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me." — John 14:6 (KJV)

> "I am the vine, ye are the branches: He that abideth in me, and I in him, the same bringeth forth much fruit: for without me ye can do nothing." — John 15:5 (KJV)
