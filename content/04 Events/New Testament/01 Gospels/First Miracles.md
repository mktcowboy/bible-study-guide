# First Miracles

## Overview
Mark's opening chapters show Jesus healing, casting out demons, cleansing a leper, and forgiving sins with authority.

## Biblical Significance
These first miracles establish the pace and theological center of [[Mark]]: the kingdom has arrived in the powerful mercy of [[Jesus Christ]].
