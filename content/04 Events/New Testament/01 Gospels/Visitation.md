# Visitation

## Overview
The Visitation is Mary's visit to Elizabeth after Gabriel announces the birth of Jesus.

## Biblical Significance
In [[Luke]] 1, Elizabeth blesses Mary, John leaps in the womb, and Mary responds with the Magnificat.

## Key People Involved

- [[Elizabeth]]

## Notes
The Visitation joins joy, prophecy, and praise at the beginning of Luke's Gospel.
