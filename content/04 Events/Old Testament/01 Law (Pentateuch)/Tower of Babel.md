# Tower of Babel

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Genesis]] 11:1-9
- **Summary:** United humanity builds a tower to reach heaven; God confuses their language and scatters them across the earth.

## Details
After the [[Flood]], all people spoke one language. Settling in Shinar, they decided: "Come, let us build us a city and a tower, whose top may reach unto heaven, and let us make us a name, lest we be scattered abroad."

This was an act of defiant pride — making a name for themselves rather than glorifying God, and resisting God's command to fill the earth. God came down, confused their language, and scattered them. The city was named Babel ("confusion").

## Key People Involved
- Descendants of [[Noah]]
- [[Nimrod]] — Traditionally associated as the builder

## Key Places
- [[Babel]] — City where the tower was built

## Important Verses
- "Come, let us build us a city and a tower, whose top may reach unto heaven, and let us make us a name, lest we be scattered abroad upon the face of the whole earth." — Genesis 11:4
