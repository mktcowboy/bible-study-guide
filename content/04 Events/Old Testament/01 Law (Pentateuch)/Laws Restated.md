# Laws Restated

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Deuteronomy]]
- **Chapters:** 4-26
- **Summary:** Moses restated the covenant law for the generation preparing to enter Canaan.

## Details
After reviewing Israel's history, Moses preached the law again as covenant instruction for life in the land. This section includes the Ten Commandments, the Shema, worship regulations, justice laws, leadership instructions, and social obligations.

## Key People Involved
- [[Moses]] — Speaker and covenant mediator
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Successor who would lead the people into the land
- [[The Elders]] — Leaders and witnesses for covenant life

## Key Themes
- Covenant obedience
- Worship centered on the Lord
- Justice and mercy in community life
