# Destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Genesis]] 18-19
- **Summary:** God destroys the wicked cities of [[Sodom]] and [[Gomorrah]] with fire and brimstone, rescuing [[Lot]] and his daughters.

## Details
After [[Abraham]] interceded (asking if God would spare the city for 50, then 45, then 20, then 10 righteous people), God determined there were not even 10 righteous in Sodom.

Two angels visited [[Lot]], who housed them. The men of Sodom demanded the visitors for sexual assault. Lot offered his daughters instead. The angels struck the attackers with blindness, then warned Lot to flee.

Lot hesitated, but angels grabbed him and his family. Lot's wife looked back and became a pillar of salt. Lot and his two daughters escaped to [[Zoar]].
