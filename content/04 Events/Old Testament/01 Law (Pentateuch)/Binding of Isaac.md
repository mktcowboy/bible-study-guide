# Binding of Isaac (Akedah)

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Genesis]] 22
- **Summary:** God tests [[Abraham]] by commanding him to sacrifice his son [[Isaac]] on Mount Moriah. Abraham obeys, but God provides a ram instead.

## Details
This is the ultimate test of faith. After 25 years of waiting for Isaac, God now asks Abraham to sacrifice him. Abraham rose early, journeyed three days, and without hesitation prepared to kill his son, trusting God could even raise the dead (Hebrews 11:19).

At the moment Abraham raised the knife, an angel stopped him. A ram caught in a thicket provided the sacrifice. God reaffirmed His covenant, swearing by Himself since there was no one greater.

Mount Moriah is traditionally identified with the Temple Mount in Jerusalem — where Solomon would later build the Temple.

## Key People Involved
- [[Abraham]] — Tested and obedient
- [[Isaac]] — Nearly sacrificed
- An angel of the LORD — Stopped Abraham

## Key Places
- Mount Moriah — Site of the test
