# Water from the Rock

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Exodus]] 17
- **Summary:** At Rephidim, the people quarrel for water. God tells Moses to strike a rock, and water flows.

## Details
Israel camped at Rephidim and there was no water. The people quarreled with Moses, saying "Wherefore is this that thou hast brought us up out of Egypt, to kill us and our children and our cattle with thirst?"

Moses cried out to the LORD. God told him to take the rod with which he smote the river, go to the rock at Horeb, and strike it. Moses struck the rock, and water came out. He called the place Massah and Meribah ("tempting" and "quarreling").

This same rock-striking event also occurred later at Kadesh (Numbers 20), where Moses struck the rock a second time — but this time he spoke angrily and was told he would not bring Israel into the Promised Land.
