# Baal Peor

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Numbers]]
- **Chapters:** Numbers 25
- **Summary:** Israel joins itself to Baal of Peor through idolatry and sexual sin, bringing judgment until [[Phinehas]] intervenes.

## Details
While Israel camps in the plains of Moab, the people are drawn into worship of Baal-peor. A plague breaks out among the people, and Phinehas turns away wrath by acting zealously for the LORD's honor.

## Key People Involved
- [[Phinehas]] — Acts zealously to stop the rebellion
- [[Moses]] — Commands judgment against the guilty leaders

## Key Places
- [[Plains of Moab]] — Israel's camp before entering Canaan

## Important Verses
> "And Israel joined himself unto Baalpeor: and the anger of the LORD was kindled against Israel." — Numbers 25:3

## Cross-References

## Notes
The episode stands near the threshold of the land as a severe warning against compromise with idolatry.
