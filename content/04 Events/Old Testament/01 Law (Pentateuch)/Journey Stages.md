# Journey Stages

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Numbers]]
- **Chapter:** 33
- **Summary:** Moses recorded Israel's wilderness itinerary from Egypt to the plains of Moab.

## Details
Numbers 33 lists the stages of Israel's journey after the Exodus. The itinerary begins at Rameses, passes through the wilderness stations of testing and provision, and ends on the plains of Moab across from Jericho.

## Key People Involved
- [[Moses]] — Recorded the journey at the Lord's command
- [[Aaron]] — Died at Mount Hor during the journey
