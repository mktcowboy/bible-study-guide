# First Service

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Leviticus]]
- **Chapter:** 9
- **Summary:** Aaron and his sons began their public priestly ministry, and fire from the Lord consumed the offerings.

## Details
After the seven-day ordination, Moses commanded Aaron to offer sacrifices for himself and for the people. Aaron obeyed, lifted his hands, and blessed Israel. The glory of the Lord appeared, and divine fire consumed the burnt offering and fat on the altar.

## Key People Involved
- [[Aaron]] — Offered the first sacrifices as high priest
- [[Moses]] — Directed the service according to God's command
- [[Nadab]] and [[Abihu]] — Present among Aaron's sons
