# Prophets and Kings

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Deuteronomy]]
- **Chapters:** 17-18
- **Summary:** Moses gave laws for Israel's future kings, priests, and prophets.

## Details
Deuteronomy anticipates Israel's future leadership in the land. Kings were to remain covenant servants under the law, not multiply horses, wives, or wealth. Priests and Levites were to minister before the Lord. Prophets were to speak God's word faithfully, with the promise that the Lord would raise up a prophet like Moses.

## Key People Involved
- [[Moses]] — Mediator of the leadership instructions
- [[The Elders]] — Local leaders responsible for justice and witness
