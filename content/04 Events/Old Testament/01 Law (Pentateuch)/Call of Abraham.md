# Call of Abraham

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Genesis]] 12:1-3
- **Summary:** God calls [[Abraham]] (then Abram) to leave [[Ur]] and go to [[Canaan]], promising to make him a great nation and bless all families of the earth.

## Details
At age 75, God called Abram with five imperatives: Get out, depart, go, I will shew thee, I will bless. With three promises attached: great nation, personal blessing, and blessing to all families of the earth.

This call required leaving everything familiar — homeland, family, and security. Abraham obeyed without knowing where he was going. This moment marks the beginning of God's redemptive plan through one family line.
