# Ten Plagues of Egypt

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Exodus]] 7:14 — 12:30
- **Summary:** God sends ten escalating judgments upon Egypt, each targeting Egyptian gods, until Pharaoh releases Israel.

## Details
[[Moses]] and [[Aaron]] confronted Pharaoh with God's demand: "Let My people go." Pharaoh refused. God sent ten plagues, each escalating in severity:

1. **Water to Blood** (Ch 7) — Nile and all water turned to blood
2. **Frogs** (Ch 8) — Frogs covered the land
3. **Gnats/Lice** (Ch 8) — Dust became gnats
4. **Flies** (Ch 8) — Swarms of flies
5. **Livestock Disease** (Ch 9) — Cattle died
6. **Boils** (Ch 9) — Painful sores on people and animals
7. **Hail** (Ch 9) — Fire and hail destroyed crops
8. **Locusts** (Ch 10) — Devoured everything remaining
9. **Darkness** (Ch 10) — Three days of palpable darkness
10. **Death of Firstborn** (Ch 12) — Every firstborn in Egypt died

After each plague, Pharaoh offered to let Israel go, then relented. God hardened his heart. Only the death of the firstborn broke his resistance.

## Key People Involved
- [[Moses]] & [[Aaron]] — Brought the plagues
- [[Pharaoh]] — Refused repeatedly
