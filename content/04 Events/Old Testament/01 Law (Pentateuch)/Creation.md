# Creation

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Genesis]] 1:1 — 2:3
- **Summary:** God creates the heavens, the earth, all life, and humanity in six days, resting on the seventh.

## Details
In six days, God created everything by His spoken word:
1. **Day 1:** Light and darkness — "Let there be light"
2. **Day 2:** Sky (firmament) separating waters
3. **Day 3:** Dry land, seas, vegetation — "Let the earth bring forth"
4. **Day 4:** Sun, moon, and stars
5. **Day 5:** Sea creatures and birds
6. **Day 6:** Land animals and humanity — created in God's image (imago Dei)
7. **Day 7:** God rested, blessed and sanctified the seventh day

Creation was declared "very good." God's method was speaking — "And God said... and it was so." The cosmos had order, purpose, and a Creator who declared His work good.

## Key People Involved
- God (Father, Son/Spirit implied in plural "Let Us make")
- [[Adam]] — Created from dust, given breath of life
- [[Eve]] — Created from Adam's rib

## Key Places
