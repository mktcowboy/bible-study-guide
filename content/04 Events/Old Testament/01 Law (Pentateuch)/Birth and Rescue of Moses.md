# Birth and Rescue of Moses

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Exodus]] 2:1-10
- **Summary:** [[Pharaoh]] orders all Hebrew baby boys killed. [[Jochebed]] hides [[Moses]] for three months, then places him in a basket on the Nile where Pharaoh's daughter finds and adopts him.

## Details
Pharaoh, fearing the growing Hebrew population, commanded midwives to kill Hebrew boys, then extended the order to all mothers. [[Jochebed]] defied the decree, hiding [[Moses]] for three months. When she could no longer hide him, she made a basket of bulrushes, coated it with pitch, and placed him among the reeds.

[[Miriam]] watched from a distance. When Pharaoh's daughter discovered the basket, Miriam approached and asked if she should call a Hebrew nurse. Pharaoh's daughter agreed, and Jochebed was hired to nurse her own son. When Moses grew, Pharaoh's daughter adopted him, calling him "Moses" ("I drew him out of the water").

## Key People Involved
- [[Moses]] — The baby
- [[Jochebed]] — Mother
- [[Miriam]] — Older sister
- Pharaoh's daughter — Rescuer
