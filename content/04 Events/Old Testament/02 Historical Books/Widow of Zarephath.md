# Widow of Zarephath

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 17
- **Summary:** During the drought, God sends [[Elijah]] to [[Zarephath]], where the widow's meal and oil do not fail and her son is later raised.

## Details
This episode pairs severe judgment on Israel with surprising mercy to a Gentile household. The story also establishes Elijah as both a prophet of drought and a prophet of life.
