# First Feast

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Esther]]
- **Chapters:** Esther 5
- **Summary:** Esther approached [[Ahasuerus]], received favor, and invited the king and [[Haman]] to a banquet before delaying her request to a second feast.

## Details
After three days of fasting, [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]] put on royal apparel and stood in the inner court. The king extended the golden scepter, sparing her life and inviting her request. Rather than reveal everything immediately, Esther invited the king and Haman to a banquet.

At the feast, the king again asked her petition, but Esther requested that they return the next day. Haman left joyful and proud, but seeing [[Mordecai]] at the gate reignited his hatred. At home, his wife and friends advised him to build gallows for Mordecai.
