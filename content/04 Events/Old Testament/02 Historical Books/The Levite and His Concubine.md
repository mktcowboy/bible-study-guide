# The Levite and His Concubine

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 19
- **Summary:** A Levite's concubine is abused and killed in [[Gibeah of Benjamin]], exposing Israel's moral collapse.

## Details
The Levite retrieves his concubine from [[Bethlehem]] and lodges in Gibeah. Men of the city commit a horrific crime, and the Levite sends pieces of the woman's body throughout Israel, summoning national outrage.

## Key People Involved
- [[Levites]] — The Levite at the center of the account
- [[Benjamin]] — Tribe implicated through Gibeah

## Key Places
- [[Bethlehem]] — Concubine's home
- [[Gibeah of Benjamin]] — Site of the atrocity

## Important Verses
> "Consider of it, take advice, and speak your minds." — Judges 19:30

## Notes
The episode deliberately echoes Sodom, showing covenant Israel acting like the cities once judged in Genesis.
