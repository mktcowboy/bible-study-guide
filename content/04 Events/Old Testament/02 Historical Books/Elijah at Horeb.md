# Elijah at Horeb

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 19
- **Summary:** After fleeing [[Jezebel]], [[Elijah]] reaches [[Mount Sinai|Horeb]] and encounters God not in wind, earthquake, or fire, but in a still small voice.

## Details
This chapter shows prophetic exhaustion after public triumph. God meets Elijah with rebuke, tenderness, recommissioning, and the assurance of a preserved remnant.
