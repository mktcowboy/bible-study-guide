# Saul & the Witch of Endor

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 28
- **Summary:** [[Saul]], abandoned by the word of the LORD, seeks forbidden counsel from a medium at [[Endor]].

## Details
Unable to receive an answer through prophets, dreams, or Urim, Saul disguises himself and turns to a practice he once condemned. The message he receives is not comfort but judgment: Israel will fall and Saul will die. The scene reveals how far the king has descended.

## Key People Involved
- [[Saul]] - Desperate king in spiritual darkness
- [[Samuel]] - Speaks judgment in the narrative

## Key Places
- [[Endor]] - Site of the forbidden consultation

## Important Verses
> "Then said Samuel, Wherefore then dost thou ask of me, seeing the LORD is departed from thee...?" - 1 Samuel 28:16

## Notes
Endor is Saul's anti-prayer scene. Instead of seeking the living God in repentance, he seeks a word through forbidden darkness.
