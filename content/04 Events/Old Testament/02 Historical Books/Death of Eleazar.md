# Death of Eleazar

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 24:33
- **Summary:** [[Eleazar]], son of [[Aaron]], dies and is buried in the hill country of Ephraim.

## Details
Eleazar served as high priest through the wilderness transition and land allotment. His death, paired with Joshua's, marks the passing of the conquest generation's leadership.

## Key People Involved
- [[Eleazar]] — High priest
- [[Aaron]] — Eleazar's father
- [[Phinehas]] — Eleazar's son

## Key Places
- [[Canaan]] — Land where Eleazar served during allotment

## Important Verses
> "And Eleazar the son of Aaron died; and they buried him..." — Joshua 24:33
