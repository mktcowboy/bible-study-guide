# David Organizes Worship

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Chronicles]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Chronicles 15-16, 23-26
- **Summary:** [[David]] appoints [[Levites]], singers, musicians, gatekeepers, and priestly orders for worship before the LORD.

## Details
This is one of the most Chronicles-specific emphases in the whole book. David is portrayed not only as warrior and king, but as liturgical organizer preparing enduring patterns of worship.
