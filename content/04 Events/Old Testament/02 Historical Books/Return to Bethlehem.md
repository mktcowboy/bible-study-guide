# Return to Bethlehem

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 1
- **Summary:** [[Naomi]] returns from [[Moab]] to [[Bethlehem]], and [[Ruth]] comes with her in covenant loyalty.

## Details
After the deaths in Moab, Naomi hears that the LORD has visited His people with bread and heads home. Ruth refuses to abandon her and enters Bethlehem as a foreigner under grace. Naomi returns calling herself Mara, but the harvest setting hints that bitterness will not be the end of the story.

## Key People Involved
- [[Naomi]] - Returns in grief
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] - Leaves Moab to remain with Naomi
- [[Orpah]] - Parts ways before the return is completed

## Key Places
- [[Moab]] - Place Naomi leaves behind
- [[Bethlehem]] - Place of return and coming restoration

## Important Verses
> "So they two went until they came to Bethlehem..." - Ruth 1:19

## Notes
The return to Bethlehem is both geographical and covenantal. Ruth comes not only to a new land, but to the people and God of Israel.
