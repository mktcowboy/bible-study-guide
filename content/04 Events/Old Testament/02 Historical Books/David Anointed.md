# David Anointed

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 16
- **Summary:** [[Samuel]] anoints [[David]] in [[Bethlehem]] after the LORD rejects Saul.

## Details
Sent to Jesse's house, Samuel first assumes Eliab is the chosen one, but the LORD teaches that He looks on the heart rather than outward appearance. David, the youngest shepherd son, is called in and anointed. The Spirit comes upon David, while Saul's decline deepens.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samuel]] - Anoints the next king
- [[David]] - Chosen by God
- [[Jesse]] - Father of David

## Key Places
- [[Bethlehem]] - Setting of the anointing

## Important Verses
> "Man looketh on the outward appearance, but the LORD looketh on the heart." - 1 Samuel 16:7

## Notes
David's anointing answers the failure of Saul. God chooses the unlikely shepherd, not the impressive eldest son.
