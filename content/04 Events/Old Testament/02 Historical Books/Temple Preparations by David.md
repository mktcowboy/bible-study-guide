# Temple Preparations by David

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Chronicles]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Chronicles 22-29
- **Summary:** [[David]] gathers materials, charges [[Solomon]], organizes personnel, and publicly gives toward the future temple.

## Details
Chronicles shifts attention away from David's failures and toward his role as the preparer for the house of God. The temple is not yet built, but its future is already being ordered by sacrifice, planning, and worship.
