# David, Nabal & Abigail

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 25
- **Summary:** [[Nabal]] insults [[David]], but [[Abigail]] intervenes with wisdom and prevents bloodshed.

## Details
After protecting Nabal's shepherds, David asks for provisions and is met with contempt. In anger David prepares to wipe out Nabal's household, but Abigail humbly meets him with gifts and counsel. David recognizes the LORD's restraint through her, and Nabal later dies under divine judgment.

## Key People Involved
- [[David]] - Tempted toward rash vengeance
- [[Nabal]] - Foolish and hardhearted
- [[Abigail]] - Wise peacemaker

## Key Places
- [[Cave of Adullam]] - Part of the broader wilderness period

## Important Verses
> "Blessed be thy advice, and blessed be thou, which hast kept me this day from coming to shed blood..." - 1 Samuel 25:33

## Notes
Abigail's wisdom guards David from becoming Saul-like. Her intervention is one of the quiet moral pivots of the book.
