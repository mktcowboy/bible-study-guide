# Solomon's Downfall

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 11
- **Summary:** [[Solomon]]'s foreign marriages turn his heart after other gods, and God announces the coming division of the kingdom.

## Details
The wisest king becomes the warning example that wisdom without covenant loyalty collapses into idolatry. The judgment is delayed for [[David]]'s sake, but the fracture is already certain.
