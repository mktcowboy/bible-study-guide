# Naboth's Vineyard

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 21
- **Summary:** [[Jezebel]] arranges the murder of [[Naboth]] so [[Ahab]] can seize his inheritance, and [[Elijah]] announces judgment on the royal house.

## Details
This narrative exposes how idolatry and injustice reinforce each other. The seizure of Naboth's vineyard is not a private crime but a royal violation of covenant order and inheritance law.
