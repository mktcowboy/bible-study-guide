# Nathan's Confrontation

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 12
- **Summary:** [[Nathan]] confronts [[David]] with the parable of the ewe lamb and declares God's judgment.

## Details
Nathan's story leads David to condemn a rich oppressor before revealing that David himself is the man. David confesses, but forgiveness does not remove temporal consequences. The rebuke explains the turmoil that will soon erupt in David's own house.
