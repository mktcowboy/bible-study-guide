# Military Victories

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 8-10
- **Summary:** [[David]] secures the kingdom through victories over surrounding nations and by wise administration.

## Details
These chapters show the outward strength of David's reign: Philistines, Moab, Aram, Edom, and Ammon are subdued or checked. The victories confirm that the LORD is establishing the kingdom He promised.
