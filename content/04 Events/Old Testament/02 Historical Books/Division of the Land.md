# Division of the Land

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 13-21
- **Summary:** The land is allotted to Israel's tribes, with cities of refuge and Levitical cities appointed.

## Details
Reuben, Gad, and half-Manasseh receive territory east of the Jordan. The western tribes receive their inheritances by lot, with [[Caleb]] receiving Hebron. The tabernacle is set up at [[Shiloh]], and the inheritance structure is completed through refuge cities and Levitical cities.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Oversees allotment
- [[Eleazar]] — Priest involved in distribution
- [[Caleb]] — Receives Hebron by faith

## Key Places
- [[Shiloh]] — Tabernacle set up
- [[Hebron]] — Caleb's inheritance
- [[Canaan]] — Land allotted
