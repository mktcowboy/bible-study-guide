# David King over Judah

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 2
- **Summary:** [[David]] is anointed king over Judah at [[Hebron]].

## Details
David does not seize the kingdom on his own terms. He first inquires of the LORD, goes to Hebron, and is anointed by the men of Judah. The event marks the beginning of his formal reign, though the kingdom is not yet united.
