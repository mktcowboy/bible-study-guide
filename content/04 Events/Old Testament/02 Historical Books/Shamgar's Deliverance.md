# Shamgar's Deliverance

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 3:31
- **Summary:** [[Shamgar]] delivers Israel by striking down six hundred Philistines with an ox goad.

## Details
The account is only one verse, but it extends the pattern of God raising unexpected deliverers during the judges era.

## Key People Involved
