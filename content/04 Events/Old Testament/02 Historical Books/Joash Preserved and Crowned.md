# Joash Preserved and Crowned

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 11
- **Summary:** The Davidic heir [[Joash]] is hidden from [[Athaliah]] and later crowned in the temple.

## Details
This chapter is a preservation story for the royal line. In a moment when Judah seems one purge away from dynastic extinction, the covenant line survives.

- [[Athaliah]]
- [[Jehoiada]]
