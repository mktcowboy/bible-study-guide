# Elisha's Miracles

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 2-8
- **Summary:** [[Elisha]]'s ministry is marked by healing, provision, resurrection, revelation, and military deliverance.

## Details
Rather than one isolated sign, [[2 Kings]] presents an extended chain of prophetic acts showing that the God of Israel is present in famine, warfare, household grief, and foreign need.
