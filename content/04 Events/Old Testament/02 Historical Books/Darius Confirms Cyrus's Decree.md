# Darius Confirms Cyrus's Decree

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ezra]]
- **Chapters:** Ezra 5-6
- **Summary:** After [[Tattenai]] investigated the resumed temple work, Darius I found [[Cyrus]]'s original decree and ordered the rebuilding to continue with royal support.

## Details
When the returned exiles resumed construction under the prophetic encouragement of [[Haggai]] and [[Zechariah]], Tattenai asked who had authorized the work and sent a formal letter to Darius. The builders appealed to Cyrus's earlier decree, so Darius commanded an archive search.

The decree was found at Ecbatana. Darius then ordered Tattenai and his associates to stay away from the work, fund it from royal tribute, and supply animals and goods for temple worship. What began as scrutiny became provision for completion.
