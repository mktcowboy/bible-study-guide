# Josiah's Reform

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 22-23
- **Summary:** [[Josiah]] responds to the rediscovered Book of the Law with covenant renewal, idol destruction, and Passover restoration.

## Details
The reform is deep and sincere, but it occurs under the shadow of already-announced judgment. It is both a renewal and a farewell blaze of covenant faithfulness.
