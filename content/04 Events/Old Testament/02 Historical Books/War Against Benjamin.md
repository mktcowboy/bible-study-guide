# War Against Benjamin

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 20-21
- **Summary:** Israel nearly destroys the tribe of Benjamin after the atrocity at Gibeah.

## Details
The tribes gather at Mizpah and demand justice, but Benjamin refuses to surrender the guilty men. Civil war follows, Benjamin is devastated, and Israel later devises troubling ways to preserve the tribe.

## Key People Involved
- [[Benjamin]] — Tribe nearly destroyed
- [[Phinehas]] — Priest consulted during the war

## Key Places

- [[Gibeah of Benjamin]] — City judged
- [[Shiloh]] — Later tied to wives for Benjamin
