# Shunammite's Son Raised

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 4
- **Summary:** [[Elisha]] raises the dead son of the hospitable woman of [[Shunem]].

## Details
This miracle turns hospitality into resurrection hope and becomes one of the most intimate scenes in Elisha's ministry.
