# Division of the Kingdom

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 12
- **Summary:** [[Rehoboam]] rejects wise counsel at [[Shechem]], and ten tribes break away under [[Jeroboam]].

## Details
What had been pronounced in [[Solomon's Downfall]] becomes public history here. The rupture is political, spiritual, and covenantal, and it shapes the rest of Israel's monarchy.
