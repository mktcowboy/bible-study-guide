# Jehu's Revolution

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 9-10
- **Summary:** [[Jehu]] is anointed and destroys the house of [[Ahab]], including [[Jezebel]], yet stops short of true covenant reform.

## Details
The purge fulfills prior prophetic judgment but also shows that violent zeal alone does not equal faithful obedience.
