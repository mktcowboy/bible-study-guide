# Saul Anointed

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 9-10
- **Summary:** [[Samuel]] privately anoints [[Saul]], and Saul is later publicly identified before Israel.

## Details
What begins with a search for lost donkeys becomes a divine appointment. Samuel pours oil on Saul, gives confirming signs, and later presents him publicly to the nation. Saul's early story carries real promise, but the larger book will show that outward stature is not enough.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samuel]] - Prophet who anoints
- [[Saul]] - First king of Israel

## Key Places
- [[Mizpah]] - Public selection by lot
- [[Gilgal]] - Kingdom later renewed there

## Important Verses
