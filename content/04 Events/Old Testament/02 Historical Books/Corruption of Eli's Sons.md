# Corruption of Eli's Sons

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 2
- **Summary:** [[Hophni & Phinehas]] profane the sacrifices and corrupt worship at [[Shiloh]], bringing judgment on Eli's house.

## Details
Eli's sons abuse priestly privilege by seizing the best of the sacrifices and behaving wickedly at the tabernacle. Their sin causes men to abhor the offering of the LORD. A prophet announces that Eli's house will be cut off because leadership tolerated evil instead of honoring God.

## Key People Involved
- [[Hophni & Phinehas]] - Corrupt priests
- [[Eli]] - Fails to restrain them
- [[Samuel]] - Contrasting child ministering before the LORD

## Key Places
- [[Shiloh]] - Center of worship being corrupted

## Important Verses
> "Wherefore the sin of the young men was very great before the LORD..." - 1 Samuel 2:17
