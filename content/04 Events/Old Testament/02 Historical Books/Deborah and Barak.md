# Deborah and Barak

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 4-5
- **Summary:** [[Deborah]] summons [[Barak]] to fight Sisera, and the LORD gives victory to Israel.

## Details
Jabin's commander [[Sisera]] oppresses Israel with iron chariots. Deborah calls Barak to Mount Tabor, the LORD routs Sisera's army, and [[Jael]] kills Sisera in her tent. Judges 5 preserves the Song of Deborah.

## Key People Involved
- [[Deborah]] — Prophetess and judge
- [[Barak]] — Military leader
- [[Sisera]] — Enemy commander
- [[Jael]] — Kills Sisera

## Key Places
- [[Mount Tabor]] — Gathering place for Barak's army

## Important Verses
> "Praise ye the LORD for the avenging of Israel, when the people willingly offered themselves." — Judges 5:2

- Related events: [[Cycle of Apostasy]]
- Mentioned in: Hebrews 11:32

## Notes
The prose account and poetic song together celebrate the LORD's victory through surprising servants.
