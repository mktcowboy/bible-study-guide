# David & Nabal-Abigail

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 25
- **Summary:** Abigail prevents David from taking vengeance on Nabal's household.

## Details
David asks Nabal for provisions after protecting his shepherds, but Nabal insults him. David prepares to kill the household. Abigail quickly brings food, appeals to David's future, and persuades him not to shed blood. God judges Nabal, and David later marries Abigail.

## Key People Involved
- [[David]] - Nearly acts in vengeance
- [[Ahinoam & Abigail]] - Abigail intercedes wisely
- Nabal - Foolish and harsh man

## Key Places
- Carmel / Maon region - Setting of the conflict

## Important Verses
- 1 Samuel 25:32-33 - David blesses Abigail's counsel.
