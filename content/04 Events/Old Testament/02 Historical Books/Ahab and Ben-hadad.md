# Ahab and Ben-hadad

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 20
- **Summary:** God delivers [[Ahab]] from [[Ben-hadad]], but Ahab spares the enemy king and comes under judgment.

## Details
The chapter highlights God's willingness to defend Israel even under an evil king, while also exposing Ahab's chronic inability to submit fully to the word of the LORD.

- [[Ben-hadad]]
