# Civil War

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 2-4
- **Summary:** The house of [[David]] and the house of [[Saul]] contend for rule through the rival claims of David and [[Ishbosheth]].

## Details
While David reigns in Judah from [[Hebron]], [[Abner]] raises up Ishbosheth across Israel from [[Mahanaim]]. Violence follows at [[Gibeon]], Abner eventually turns toward David, and Joab murders him in revenge. The weakening of Saul's house ends when Ishbosheth is assassinated and David rejects the bloodguilt.
