# Mercy to Mephibosheth

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 9
- **Summary:** [[David]] shows covenant kindness to [[Mephibosheth]], the son of Jonathan.

## Details
Instead of eliminating a rival line, David seeks someone from Saul's house to whom he can show kindness for Jonathan's sake. Mephibosheth is welcomed to eat continually at the king's table, turning weakness and fear into protected belonging.

This is one of the clearest scenes of covenant mercy in David's life and a major counterweight to the darker royal narratives.
