# Davidic Covenant

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 7
- **Summary:** Through [[Nathan]], God promises [[David]] an enduring house, kingdom, and throne.

## Details
David desires to build a house for the LORD, but God reverses the language and promises to build a house for David. The covenant includes an immediate royal line and ultimately points beyond Solomon to the everlasting king.
