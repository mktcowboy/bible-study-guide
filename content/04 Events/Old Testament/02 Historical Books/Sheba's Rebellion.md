# Sheba's Rebellion

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 20
- **Summary:** [[Sheba]] launches another revolt after Absalom's fall, and the uprising is crushed through ruthless action.

## Details
The kingdom's divisions flare again almost immediately. [[Amasa]] delays, [[Joab]] murders him, and the pursuit ends only when a wise woman negotiates Sheba's death. The revolt reveals how unstable the restored kingdom still is.
