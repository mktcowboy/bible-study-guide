# Battle of Ai

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 8
- **Summary:** After Achan's sin is judged, Israel defeats [[Ai]] through an ambush commanded by the LORD.

## Details
Joshua sends part of the army behind the city while the main force draws Ai's men away. At the LORD's command Joshua stretches out his spear, the ambush rises, and Ai is destroyed. The covenant is then read at Mount Ebal and Mount Gerizim.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leads the ambush
- [[Achan]] — His earlier sin explains the first defeat

## Key Places
- [[Ai]] — City defeated after Israel's repentance
- [[Mount Ebal & Gerizim]] — Covenant ceremony follows the battle

## Important Verses
