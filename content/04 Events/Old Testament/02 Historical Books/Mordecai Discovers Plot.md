# Mordecai Discovers Plot

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Esther]]
- **Chapters:** Esther 2:19-23
- **Summary:** [[Mordecai]] uncovered a plot by [[Bigthan & Teresh]] against [[Ahasuerus]], and the report was recorded in the royal chronicles.

## Details
While Mordecai sat at the [[King's Gate]], two royal chamberlains became angry with Ahasuerus and planned to attack the king. Mordecai learned of the conspiracy and told [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]], who reported it to the king in Mordecai's name.

The matter was investigated and found true, and the conspirators were hanged. Mordecai's service was written in the book of the chronicles but not immediately rewarded, creating the hidden setup for [[Mordecai Honored]].

## Key People Involved
- [[Mordecai]] - Discovered the plot
- [[Esther (Queen)|Esther]] - Reported it in Mordecai's name
- [[Bigthan & Teresh]] - Palace officials who plotted against the king
- [[Ahasuerus]] - Target of the conspiracy
