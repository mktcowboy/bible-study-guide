# Angel of the LORD at Bochim

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 2:1-5
- **Summary:** The angel of the LORD rebukes Israel for covenant compromise, and the people weep at [[Bochim]].

## Details
The LORD reminds Israel that He brought them from Egypt and promised never to break His covenant. Because Israel has made covenants with the inhabitants of the land and has not thrown down their altars, those peoples will become snares.
