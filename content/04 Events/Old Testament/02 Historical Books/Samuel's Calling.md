# Samuel's Calling

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 3
- **Summary:** The LORD calls the boy [[Samuel]] at night and entrusts him with a word of judgment against Eli's house.

## Details
While serving before the LORD at [[Shiloh]], Samuel hears his name called and mistakes the voice for [[Eli]]. Eli eventually realizes the LORD is speaking and instructs Samuel to answer. Samuel receives a hard first message, but from that point Israel recognizes him as a true prophet.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samuel]] - Called as prophet
- [[Eli]] - Helps Samuel understand the call

## Key Places
- [[Shiloh]] - Setting of the call

## Important Verses
> "Speak; for thy servant heareth." - 1 Samuel 3:10

- Related events: [[Hannah's Prayer]], [[Corruption of Eli's Sons]]

## Notes
Samuel's call is both tender and severe. God raises a new prophetic voice while announcing the collapse of a corrupt priestly house.
