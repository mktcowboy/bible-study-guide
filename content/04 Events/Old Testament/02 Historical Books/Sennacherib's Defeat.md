# Sennacherib's Defeat

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 18-19
- **Summary:** [[Sennacherib]] threatens [[Jerusalem]], but the LORD delivers Judah in response to [[Hezekiah]]'s prayer and [[Isaiah (Prophet)|Isaiah]]'s prophecy.

## Details
The crisis becomes a theological showdown between imperial arrogance and the living God. Jerusalem survives not through military strength but through divine intervention.
