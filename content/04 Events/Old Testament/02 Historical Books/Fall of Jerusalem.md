# Fall of Jerusalem

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 24-25
- **Summary:** Babylon conquers Judah, destroys [[Jerusalem]] and the temple, and carries the people into exile.

## Details
The book's longest trajectory lands here. What was warned in Torah, foreshadowed in kings, and announced by prophets becomes historical devastation.
