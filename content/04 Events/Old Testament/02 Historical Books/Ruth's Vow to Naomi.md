# Ruth's Vow to Naomi

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 1
- **Summary:** [[Ruth]] clings to [[Naomi]] and pledges herself to Naomi's people and God.

## Details
After the deaths of their husbands, Naomi urges Ruth and Orpah to return to Moab. Orpah leaves, but Ruth refuses to abandon Naomi and returns with her to [[Bethlehem]].

## Key People Involved
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] — Makes the vow
- [[Naomi]] — Returns to Bethlehem in grief

## Key Places
