# Temple Dedication

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 8
- **Summary:** [[Solomon]] brings the ark into the temple, prays for covenant mercy, and the glory cloud fills the house.

## Details
The dedication is the theological high point of [[1 Kings]]. [[Solomon]] acknowledges that heaven cannot contain God, yet pleads that the temple will be the place where repentant prayers are heard.
