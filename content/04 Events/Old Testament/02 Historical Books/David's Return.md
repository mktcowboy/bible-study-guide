# David's Return

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 19
- **Summary:** After Absalom's defeat, [[David]] returns to the kingdom and re-enters [[Jerusalem]] in a humbled, divided atmosphere.

## Details
Joab first rebukes David for excessive mourning, then the king is slowly restored by Judah and the tribes. Old wounds surface on the way back: [[Shimei]] begs mercy, [[Mephibosheth]] is heard, and division between Judah and Israel remains unresolved.
