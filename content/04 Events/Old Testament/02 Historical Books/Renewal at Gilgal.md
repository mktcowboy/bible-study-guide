# Renewal at Gilgal

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 5
- **Summary:** Israel renews covenant signs at [[Gilgal]] through circumcision and Passover before beginning the conquest.

## Details
The wilderness generation's sons are circumcised, the reproach of Egypt is rolled away, Passover is kept in the land, and the manna ceases as Israel eats the produce of Canaan. Joshua then encounters the [[Commander of the LORD's Army]].

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Oversees covenant renewal
- [[Commander of the LORD's Army]] — Appears before Jericho

## Key Places
- [[Gilgal]] — First major camp in Canaan
- [[Canaan]] — Land whose produce Israel begins to eat

## Important Verses
> "This day have I rolled away the reproach of Egypt from off you." — Joshua 5:9
