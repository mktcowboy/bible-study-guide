# Spies Sent to Jericho

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 2
- **Summary:** Joshua sends two spies into [[Jericho]], where [[Rahab]] hides them and confesses faith in Israel's God.

## Details
Rahab protects the spies from Jericho's king, asks for mercy for her household, and receives the scarlet cord as a sign. The spies return with a faithful report: the land is fearful because the LORD has given it to Israel.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Sends the spies
- [[Rahab]] — Protects the spies and seeks mercy

## Key Places
- [[Jericho]] — First fortified city before Israel
- [[Jordan River]] — Boundary crossed by the spies

## Important Verses
> "For the LORD your God, he is God in heaven above, and in earth beneath." — Joshua 2:11

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Fall of Jericho]], [[Crossing the Jordan]]
- Mentioned in: Hebrews 11:31, James 2:25

## Notes
Rahab's confession stands in contrast to Canaan's fear: she responds to the same report with faith.
