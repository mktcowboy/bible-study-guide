# Saul Spares Agag

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 15
- **Summary:** [[Saul]] spares [[Agag]] and the best spoil of Amalek, leading to his rejection as king.

## Details
Saul is commanded to devote Amalek to destruction, but he preserves Agag and valuable livestock. When confronted, he cloaks disobedience in religious language. Samuel responds with the defining rebuke of the book: obedience is better than sacrifice.

## Key People Involved
- [[Saul]] - Disobeys God's command
- [[Samuel]] - Confronts and rejects Saul
- [[Agag]] - Enemy king wrongly spared

## Key Places
- [[Gilgal]] - Place of confrontation

## Important Verses

## Notes
This chapter is the hinge of Saul's story. His kingdom is not lost through weakness alone, but through selective obedience dressed up as devotion.
