# Ark Brought to Jerusalem

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 6
- **Summary:** [[David]] brings the ark to [[Jerusalem]], but the celebration is interrupted by judgment on Uzzah and later by Michal's contempt.

## Details
David desires the LORD's presence at the center of the kingdom, yet the first attempt reveals that holy things cannot be handled casually. After a period of fear and reflection, David brings the ark into Jerusalem with sacrifice and rejoicing.
