# Gleaning in the Field

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 2
- **Summary:** [[Ruth]] gleans in the field of [[Boaz]], and God's providence quietly guides the meeting.

## Details
Seeking food for herself and [[Naomi]], Ruth goes out to gather what harvesters leave behind for the poor. What seems like chance leads her to Boaz's field. The scene highlights obedience to the law's provision for the needy while revealing divine providence under ordinary labor.

## Key People Involved
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] - Faithfully gleans for survival
- [[Boaz]] - Owner of the field
- [[Naomi]] - Receives the gathered grain

## Key Places
- [[Bethlehem]] - Town tied to the harvest

## Important Verses
