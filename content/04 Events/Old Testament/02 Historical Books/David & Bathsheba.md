# David & Bathsheba

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 11
- **Summary:** [[David]] commits adultery with [[Bathsheba]] and arranges the death of [[Uriah the Hittite]].

## Details
The king who would not seize Saul's throne by violence now abuses his own power from the rooftop of the palace. The sin deepens from desire to deception to murder, and the strength of David's public kingdom is suddenly overshadowed by corruption at the center.
