# Cities of Refuge

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 20
- **Summary:** Six cities are appointed as refuge for anyone who kills another unintentionally.

## Details
The refuge system protects the manslayer from blood vengeance until a fair hearing. Three cities stand west of the Jordan and three east of it, making mercy accessible throughout Israel's inheritance.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Establishes the cities
- [[Eleazar]] — Priest associated with land allotment

## Key Places
- [[Canaan]] — Western refuge cities
- [[Jordan River]] — Boundary dividing the cities

## Important Verses
> "That the slayer that killeth any person unawares and unwittingly may flee thither..." — Joshua 20:3

## Notes
Justice in the land includes both punishment for guilt and protection against vengeance.
