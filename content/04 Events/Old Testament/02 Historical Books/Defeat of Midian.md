# Defeat of Midian

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 7
- **Summary:** The LORD reduces Gideon's army to three hundred so Israel will know the victory is God's.

## Details
God dismisses the fearful and then reduces the army again at the water test. Gideon's three hundred men carry trumpets, jars, and torches; the Midianite camp panics and turns on itself.

## Key People Involved
- [[Gideon]] — Leads the three hundred
