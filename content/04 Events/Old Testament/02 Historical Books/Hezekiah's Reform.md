# Hezekiah's Reform

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 18
- **Summary:** [[Hezekiah]] removes high places, destroys idolatrous objects, and trusts the LORD.

## Details
This is one of Judah's clearest reform movements. Even the [[Bronze Serpent]] is destroyed when it has become an object of false devotion.
