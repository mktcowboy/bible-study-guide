# Northern Campaign

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 11
- **Summary:** [[Jabin]] of [[Hazor]] gathers a northern coalition, but the LORD gives them into Joshua's hand.

## Details
The northern armies gather at the waters of Merom with horses and chariots. The LORD tells Joshua not to fear. Israel attacks suddenly, disables the horses, burns the chariots, and burns Hazor, the head of those kingdoms.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leads Israel
- [[Jabin]] — King of Hazor and coalition leader

## Key Places
- [[Hazor]] — Chief northern city
- [[Canaan]] — Conquest extends across the land

## Important Verses
> "Be not afraid because of them: for to morrow about this time will I deliver them up all slain before Israel." — Joshua 11:6

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Southern Campaign]], [[Division of the Land]]
- Connected to: Judges 4

## Notes
Hazor appears again in Judges, showing that Joshua's victories must be followed by continuing covenant faithfulness.
