# Mount Carmel Showdown

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 18
- **Summary:** [[Elijah]] confronts the prophets of Baal on [[Mount Carmel]], and the LORD answers by fire before all Israel.

## Details
The contest exposes Baal's silence and God's living power. It is the decisive public collision between covenant faithfulness and state-sponsored idolatry in [[1 Kings]].
