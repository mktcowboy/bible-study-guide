# Micaiah and Ahab's Death

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 22
- **Summary:** [[Micaiah son of Imlah]] foretells disaster, but [[Ahab]] goes to [[Ramoth-gilead]] anyway and dies in battle.

## Details
The end of [[1 Kings]] closes the Ahab cycle with a choice between flattering consensus and costly truth. Ahab's disguise cannot evade the word already spoken.
