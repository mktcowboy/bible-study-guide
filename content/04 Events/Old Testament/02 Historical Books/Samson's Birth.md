# Samson's Birth

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 13
- **Summary:** Samson's birth is announced to [[Manoah]] and his wife, and he is set apart as a Nazirite from the womb.

## Details
The angel of the LORD announces that the child will begin to deliver Israel from the Philistines. Manoah asks for instruction, offers a sacrifice, and realizes they have seen a divine messenger.

## Key People Involved
- [[Manoah]] — Samson's father
- [[Samson]] — Child promised by the messenger

## Key Places
- [[Zorah]] — Samson's family setting

## Important Verses
> "For the child shall be a Nazarite unto God from the womb..." — Judges 13:5

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Samson's Exploits]], [[Samson's Downfall]]

## Notes
Samson is set apart before birth, but his life will show the tension between divine calling and personal appetite.
