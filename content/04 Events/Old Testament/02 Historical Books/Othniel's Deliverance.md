# Othniel's Deliverance

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 3:7-11
- **Summary:** The Spirit of the LORD comes upon [[Othniel]], and he delivers Israel from oppression.

## Details
Israel serves other gods and is sold into the hand of Cushan-rishathaim for eight years. When the people cry out, the LORD raises Othniel, who judges Israel and brings forty years of rest.

## Key People Involved
- [[Othniel]] — Spirit-empowered deliverer

## Key Places
- [[Canaan]] — Land where Israel is oppressed and delivered

## Important Verses
> "And the Spirit of the LORD came upon him, and he judged Israel..." — Judges 3:10
