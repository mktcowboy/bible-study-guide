# The Threshing Floor

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 3
- **Summary:** [[Ruth]] approaches [[Boaz]] at the threshing floor and appeals for redemption.

## Details
Following Naomi's counsel, Ruth goes to Boaz after the barley winnowing and asks him to spread his skirt over her, a symbolic request for covenant covering and marriage. Boaz responds honorably, praises Ruth's virtue, and promises to settle the matter with the closer kinsman.

## Key People Involved
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] - Makes the appeal for redemption
- [[Boaz]] - Responds with integrity
- [[Naomi]] - Guides Ruth's actions
