# Ark Captured

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 4
- **Summary:** Israel is defeated by the Philistines, the ark is captured, and [[Eli]] dies after hearing the news.

## Details
Treating the ark like a battle talisman rather than a sign of covenant holiness, Israel brings it into camp against the Philistines. The result is disaster: Hophni and Phinehas die, the ark is taken, and Eli falls dead when he hears the report. The birth of [[Ichabod]] seals the moment with the cry that the glory has departed.

## Key People Involved
- [[Eli]] - Dies after the news
- [[Hophni & Phinehas]] - Killed in battle
- [[Ichabod]] - His name memorializes the loss

## Notes
The capture of the ark is not the defeat of God, but the judgment of a people who tried to use holy things without holy obedience.
