# Death of Joshua

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 24:29-31
- **Summary:** [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] dies at 110 and is buried in [[Timnathserah]].

## Details
Joshua's death closes the conquest generation. Israel serves the LORD during Joshua's lifetime and during the lifetime of the elders who knew the LORD's works for Israel.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leader of Israel
- [[Eleazar]] — High priest who also dies in the closing notices

## Key Places
- [[Timnathserah]] — Joshua's inheritance and burial place
- [[Shechem]] — Covenant renewal shortly before his death

## Important Verses
> "And Israel served the LORD all the days of Joshua..." — Joshua 24:31

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Covenant Renewal at Shechem]], [[Death of Eleazar]]
- Followed by: [[Judges]]

## Notes
The note about the elders prepares for Judges, where the next generation does not know the LORD's works in the same way.
