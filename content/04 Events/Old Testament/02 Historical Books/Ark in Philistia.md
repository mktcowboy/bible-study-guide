# Ark in Philistia

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 5-6
- **Summary:** The captured ark brings plagues and humiliation to the Philistines until they return it to Israel.

## Details
The Philistines place the ark beside Dagon, but their god falls before it and the land is struck with judgment. City after city suffers until the Philistines send the ark back on a new cart with guilt offerings. The episode proves the LORD's sovereignty even when His people have been faithless.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samuel]] - Emerging leader in the era after the ark's return

## Key Places
- [[Gath]] - One of the Philistine cities afflicted

## Important Verses
> "The hand of the LORD was heavy upon them of Ashdod..." - 1 Samuel 5:6

## Notes
Israel may have lost the battle, but the Philistines never truly possess the LORD's ark. The narrative mocks idol power and vindicates God's holiness.
