# Samson's Downfall

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 16
- **Summary:** [[Delilah]] betrays [[Samson]], leading to his capture, blindness, final prayer, and death.

## Details
Philistine rulers bribe Delilah to discover Samson's strength. Samson finally reveals the sign of his Nazirite consecration, is shorn, captured, blinded, and imprisoned. In his final act, he prays for strength and brings down the Philistine temple.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samson]] — Captured and dies in final judgment
- [[Delilah]] — Betrays him

## Key Places
- [[Valley of Sorek]] — Delilah's setting

## Important Verses
