# Solomon's Wisdom

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 3
- **Summary:** At [[Gibeon]], [[Solomon]] asks for an understanding heart instead of long life, riches, or vengeance.

## Details
God invites Solomon to ask, and the king's request reveals both humility and covenant responsibility. The answer shapes the entire first half of [[1 Kings]], because Solomon's glory is inseparable from wisdom given by God.
