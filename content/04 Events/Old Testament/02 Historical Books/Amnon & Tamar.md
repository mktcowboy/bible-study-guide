# Amnon & Tamar

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 13
- **Summary:** [[Amnon]] violates [[Tamar]], and the fracture in David's house deepens into hatred and revenge.

## Details
What Nathan warned about begins to unfold inside the royal family. Tamar is shamed, Amnon hardens into cruelty, David does not act decisively, and [[Absalom]] quietly stores up vengeance that will later burst into open revolt.
