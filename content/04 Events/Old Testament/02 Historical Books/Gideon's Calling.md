# Gideon's Calling

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 6
- **Summary:** The LORD calls [[Gideon]] while he hides from Midian, naming him a mighty man of valor.

## Details
Gideon threshes wheat in a winepress because of Midianite oppression. The angel of the LORD commissions him, and Gideon destroys his father's Baal altar at night.

## Key People Involved
- [[Gideon]] — Reluctant deliverer

## Key Places
- [[Ophrah]] — Gideon's hometown

## Important Verses
> "Go in this thy might, and thou shalt save Israel from the hand of the Midianites..." — Judges 6:14

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Wool Test]], [[Defeat of Midian]]

## Notes
Gideon's weakness is not denied; it is the stage on which God's strength is shown.
