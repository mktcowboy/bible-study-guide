## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 20
- **Summary:** [[Hezekiah]] prays and receives fifteen additional years, but Babylonian attention foreshadows future exile.

The chapter combines mercy and warning. Life is extended, yet the seeds of later judgment are plainly spoken.
