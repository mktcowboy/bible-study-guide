# Danite Migration

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 18
- **Summary:** Danite scouts seize Micah's shrine and establish idolatrous worship in [[Laish-Dan]].

## Details
The Danites seek territory, find Laish secure and isolated, then steal Micah's idol and priest. They conquer Laish, rename it Dan, and set up the carved image.

## Key People Involved
- [[Micah of Ephraim]] — Loses his shrine
- [[Levites]] — A Levite becomes priest for Dan

## Key Places
- [[Laish-Dan]] — Captured and renamed by Dan
- [[Mount Ephraim]] — Starting point of the stolen shrine

## Important Verses
> "And the children of Dan set up the graven image..." — Judges 18:30

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Micah's Idolatry]]

## Notes
The tribe that struggled to possess its inheritance ends by creating a rival religious center.
