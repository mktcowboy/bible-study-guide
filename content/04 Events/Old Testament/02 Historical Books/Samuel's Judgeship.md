# Samuel's Judgeship

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 7
- **Summary:** [[Samuel]] calls Israel to repentance at [[Mizpah]], intercedes for them, and marks victory with the stone of [[Ebenezer]].

## Details
Samuel tells Israel to put away foreign gods and return wholeheartedly to the LORD. As he offers sacrifice and cries out for the people, God thunders against the Philistines and throws them into confusion. Samuel then erects the Ebenezer memorial, declaring that the LORD has helped them.

## Key People Involved
- [[Samuel]] - Judge, prophet, and intercessor

## Key Places
- [[Mizpah]] - Assembly for repentance
- [[Ebenezer]] - Memorial of divine help
- [[Ramah]] - Samuel's home and judging center

## Important Verses
