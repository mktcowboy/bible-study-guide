# David King over All Israel

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 5
- **Summary:** All Israel comes to [[David]], who is anointed king over the whole nation and captures [[Jerusalem]].

## Details
The elders acknowledge David as shepherd and ruler, and the kingdom is finally united under one king. David then takes the stronghold of [[Jerusalem (Zion)]], establishing the city that will define his reign.
