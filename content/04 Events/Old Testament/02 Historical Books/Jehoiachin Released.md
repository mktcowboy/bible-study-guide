# Jehoiachin Released

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 25:27-30
- **Summary:** Exiled king [[Jehoiachin]] is lifted up in Babylon, providing the book's final note of restrained hope.

## Details
The closing scene does not erase the exile, but it refuses to let judgment have the final theological word. The Davidic line still lives.
