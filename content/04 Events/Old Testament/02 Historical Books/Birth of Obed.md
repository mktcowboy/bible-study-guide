# Birth of Obed

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 4
- **Summary:** [[Ruth]] and [[Boaz]] have a son, Obed, who becomes the grandfather of [[David]].

## Details
After Boaz redeems Ruth and Naomi's family line, the women of Bethlehem bless the LORD for providing a redeemer. Obed's birth turns Naomi's emptiness into restoration and carries the line toward David and ultimately [[Jesus Christ]].

## Key People Involved
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] — Mother of Obed
- [[Boaz]] — Father of Obed
- [[Naomi]] — Receives restoration through Obed's birth
- [[David]] — Grandson of Obed

## Key Places
- [[Bethlehem]] — Setting of the birth and genealogy

## Important Verses
