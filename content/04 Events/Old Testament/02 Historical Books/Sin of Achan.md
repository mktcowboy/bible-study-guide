# Sin of Achan

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 7
- **Summary:** [[Achan]] takes devoted things from Jericho, causing Israel's defeat at [[Ai]].

## Details
Israel assumes Ai will be easy, but the army is routed because covenant sin is hidden in the camp. The LORD exposes Achan by lot; Achan confesses that he coveted and took silver, gold, and a Babylonian garment.

## Key People Involved
- [[Achan]] — Takes the devoted things
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Intercedes and administers judgment

## Key Places
- [[Ai]] — Site of Israel's defeat
- [[Valley of Achor]] — Place of judgment

## Important Verses
> "Israel hath sinned, and they have also transgressed my covenant..." — Joshua 7:11

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Fall of Jericho]], [[Battle of Ai]]
- Foreshadowed in: [[Blessings and Curses Proclaimed]]

## Notes
Ai teaches that Israel's danger is not merely outside the camp; covenant unfaithfulness within the camp can undo public victory.
