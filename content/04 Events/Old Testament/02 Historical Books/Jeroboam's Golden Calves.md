# Jeroboam's Golden Calves

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 12-13
- **Summary:** [[Jeroboam]] establishes rival shrines at [[Bethel]] and [[Laish-Dan|Dan]], setting the northern kingdom on a lasting path of idolatry.

## Details
Jeroboam fears losing political control if the people continue to worship in [[Jerusalem]]. His alternative priesthood and feast structure are politically shrewd but spiritually disastrous.
