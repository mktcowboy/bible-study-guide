# Jephthah's Vow

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 11
- **Summary:** [[Jephthah]] vows rashly before battle and suffers tragedy after victory.

## Details
Jephthah debates the Ammonite claim to Israel's land, the Spirit of the LORD comes upon him, and he defeats Ammon. His vow concerning whoever comes out of his house first leads to grief over his daughter.

## Key People Involved
- [[Jephthah]] — Makes the vow and wins the battle
