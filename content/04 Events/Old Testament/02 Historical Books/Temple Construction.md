# Temple Construction

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 5-7
- **Summary:** [[Solomon]], aided by [[Hiram of Tyre]], builds the house of the LORD in [[Jerusalem]].

## Details
This section marks the architectural and covenant center of the united monarchy. The temple gathers together kingship, worship, memory of the exodus, and the hope that God's name will dwell among His people.

## Key People Involved
- [[Solomon]]
- [[Hiram of Tyre]]

## Key Places
- [[Jerusalem]]
- [[Tyre]]
