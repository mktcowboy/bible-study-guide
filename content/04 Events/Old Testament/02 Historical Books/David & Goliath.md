# David & Goliath

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Samuel 17
- **Summary:** [[David]] defeats [[Goliath]] of [[Gath]] with a sling and stone in the name of the LORD.

## Details
While Israel and Saul are paralyzed by fear, David sees Goliath's challenge as defiance against the living God. Rejecting Saul's armor, he goes forward with faith and a shepherd's sling. The giant falls, and Israel learns again that victory belongs to the LORD.

## Key People Involved
- [[David]] - Young champion of faith
- [[Goliath]] - Philistine giant
- [[Saul]] - King unable to act

## Key Places
- [[Gath]] - Goliath's home city
- [[Bethlehem]] - David's home background
