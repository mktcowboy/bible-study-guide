# Absalom's Rebellion

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 15-18
- **Summary:** [[Absalom]] steals the hearts of Israel, seizes [[Jerusalem]], and drives [[David]] into flight.

## Details
Absalom's rebellion is both political and personal. David leaves the city in grief, crosses the [[Kidron Brook]], ascends the [[Mount of Olives]], and depends on loyal friends like [[Hushai]], [[Ittai]], and [[Zadok]]. The rebellion ends in the forest with Absalom's death under the hand of [[Joab]].
