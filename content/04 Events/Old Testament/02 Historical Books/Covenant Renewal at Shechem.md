# Covenant Renewal at Shechem

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 24
- **Summary:** Joshua gathers Israel at [[Shechem]], recounts God's saving acts, and calls the nation to serve the LORD alone.

## Details
Joshua retells Israel's story from Abraham to the conquest, emphasizing grace before command. Israel vows to serve the LORD, Joshua warns them of covenant seriousness, and a stone is raised as witness.

## Key People Involved
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Leads the renewal
- [[Abraham]] — Starting point of the covenant story

## Key Places
- [[Shechem]] — Covenant assembly site
- [[Canaan]] — Inheritance now possessed

## Important Verses
> "Choose you this day whom ye will serve... but as for me and my house, we will serve the LORD." — Joshua 24:15
