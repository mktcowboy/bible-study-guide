# Levitical Cities

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Joshua]]
- **Chapters:** Joshua 21
- **Summary:** Forty-eight cities are given to the [[Levites]] from the inheritances of the tribes.

## Details
Because the Levites receive the LORD as their inheritance rather than a tribal territory, they are given cities and pasturelands throughout Israel. This places priestly and teaching presence across the land.

## Key People Involved
- [[Eleazar]] — High priest
- [[Joshua (Son of Nun)]] — Land distribution leader

## Key Places
- [[Shiloh]] — Central worship site during allotment
- [[Canaan]] — Land where Levitical cities are distributed

## Important Verses
> "And the LORD gave unto Israel all the land which he sware to give unto their fathers..." — Joshua 21:43

## Cross-References
- Related events: [[Division of the Land]], [[Cities of Refuge]]
- Foreshadowed in: Numbers 35

## Notes
The Levitical cities show that worship and instruction belong throughout Israel, not only at the central sanctuary.
