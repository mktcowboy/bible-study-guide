# Death of Saul Reported

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 1
- **Summary:** An Amalekite brings [[David]] news of [[Saul]] and [[Jonathan]]'s deaths and claims to have killed Saul.

## Details
The messenger expects reward, but his story instead becomes grounds for execution. David mourns deeply for Saul and Jonathan, showing reverence for the LORD's anointed and refusing to celebrate the fall of his enemy.
