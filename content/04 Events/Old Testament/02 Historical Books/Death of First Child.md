# Death of First Child

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 12
- **Summary:** The first child born to [[David]] and [[Bathsheba]] dies under the announced judgment of God.

## Details
David fasts, prays, and lies on the ground for the child, but the judgment falls as Nathan said. David then rises, worships, and receives the mystery of God's justice and mercy without bitterness. Later [[Solomon]] is born.

This is one of the most sobering consequence passages in David's life. Forgiveness is real, but sin still wounds the house.
