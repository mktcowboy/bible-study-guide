# Ahaziah and Baal-zebub

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 1
- **Summary:** King Ahaziah seeks an oracle from Baal-zebub at [[Ekron]], and [[Elijah]] answers with judgment and fire.

## Details
The book opens by exposing how deeply Israel's kingship has collapsed. The issue is not only Ahaziah's injury but his refusal to seek the LORD.
