# Micah's Idolatry

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Judges]]
- **Chapters:** Judges 17
- **Summary:** [[Micah of Ephraim]] builds a private shrine, revealing Israel's religious disorder.

## Details
Micah returns stolen silver to his mother, and some of it becomes an idol. He makes an ephod and household gods, installs his son as priest, then hires a wandering Levite.

## Key People Involved
- [[Micah of Ephraim]] — Shrine owner
- [[Levites]] — A Levite serves in the shrine

## Key Places
- [[Mount Ephraim]] — Setting of Micah's household shrine

## Important Verses
> "In those days there was no king in Israel..." — Judges 17:6
