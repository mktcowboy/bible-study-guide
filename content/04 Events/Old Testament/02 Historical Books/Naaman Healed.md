# Naaman Healed

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 5
- **Summary:** [[Naaman]] is healed of leprosy after obeying [[Elisha]] and washing in the [[Jordan River]].

## Details
The story exposes pride, rewards humility, and shows that grace is not controlled by national boundaries or social rank.
