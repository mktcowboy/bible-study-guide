# Uzza and the Ark

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Chronicles]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Chronicles 13
- **Summary:** [[Uzza]] dies when he reaches out to steady the ark, teaching [[David]] and Israel the holiness of God's presence.

## Details
The event interrupts celebration with fear. Chronicles uses it to stress that worship must be joyful, but never careless.
