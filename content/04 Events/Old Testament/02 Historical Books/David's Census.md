# David's Census

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Samuel]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Samuel 24
- **Summary:** [[David]] orders a census in pride, bringing plague until mercy is shown at [[Araunah's Threshing Floor]].

## Details
Despite [[Joab]]'s warning, David insists on numbering the people. Conviction follows too late, and judgment spreads through the land until God halts the plague at Araunah's threshing floor. There David offers costly worship, and the book ends with mercy.
