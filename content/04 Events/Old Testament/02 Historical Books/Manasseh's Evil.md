# Manasseh's Evil

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[2 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 2 Kings 21
- **Summary:** [[Manasseh]] fills Judah with idolatry and bloodshed, and judgment is framed as certain.

## Details
The chapter explains why later reforms, however sincere, do not simply erase the accumulated moral collapse of Judah.
