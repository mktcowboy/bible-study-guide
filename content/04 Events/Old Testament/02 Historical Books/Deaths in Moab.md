# Deaths in Moab

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Ruth]]
- **Chapters:** Ruth 1
- **Summary:** [[Elimelech]] dies in [[Moab]], and later both [[Mahlon & Chilion]] also die, leaving [[Naomi]], [[Ruth]], and [[Orpah]] widowed.

## Details
What began as a temporary sojourn for survival turns into a house of mourning. Elimelech dies first, and after the sons marry in Moab, both sons die without children. These losses create the emptiness that defines Naomi's return and make the need for a redeemer central to the rest of the book.

## Key People Involved
- [[Elimelech]] - Naomi's husband, dies in Moab
- [[Naomi]] - Left bereaved
- [[Mahlon & Chilion]] - Sons who also die in Moab
- [[Ruth (Person)|Ruth]] - Left a widow
- [[Orpah]] - Left a widow

## Key Places
