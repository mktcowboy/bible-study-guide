# Solomon's Succession

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[1 Kings]]
- **Chapters:** 1 Kings 1-2
- **Summary:** [[Adonijah]] attempts to seize the throne, but [[David]]'s promise is publicly secured as [[Solomon]] is anointed at [[Gihon]].

## Details
The opening of [[1 Kings]] is a tense royal transition. [[Nathan]] and [[Bathsheba]] move decisively, [[Zadok]] anoints Solomon, and the rival gathering at [[En-rogel]] collapses. Chapter 2 then shows Solomon consolidating the kingdom by dealing with lingering threats.
