# Chaldean Princes

## Overview
- **Testament:** Old
- **Book:** [[Jeremiah]]
- **Chapters:** Jeremiah 39
- **Summary:** Babylonian officials entered Jerusalem after the breach of the city and carried out the transfer of power as Judah collapsed.

## Details
When Jerusalem's wall was breached, the princes of Babylon entered and sat in the middle gate. Their presence marked the visible end of Judah's independent rule and the beginning of Babylonian control over the ruined city.

In the same chapter, [[Jeremiah (Prophet)|Jeremiah]] was released by Babylonian command while [[Zedekiah]] was captured and judged. The contrast is sharp: the prophet rejected by Judah is preserved by the conquering empire.
